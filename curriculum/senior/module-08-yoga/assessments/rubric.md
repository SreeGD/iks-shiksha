# Paper Rubric — Module 8 (Senior)

**Paper marks:** 65 (with summative 35 → 100 total).

## Criteria

| Criterion | 4 | 3 | 2 | 1 | Weight |
|-----------|---|---|---|---|--------|
| **Thesis clarity** | Crystal-clear, specific, defensible | Clear | Vague | None | × 3 |
| **Classical source** | YS or BG verse quoted in IAST + translation + interpreted | Cites verse, interprets adequately | Vague | None | × 2 |
| **Historical source** | Singleton or De Michelis cited substantively | Cites scholarship | Vague | None | × 2 |
| **Modern research** | Peer-reviewed study cited and critiqued | Study cited | Vague reference | None | × 2 |
| **Counter-argument** | Steel-manned opposing view + reasoned response | Counter present | Acknowledged | Strawmanned | × 2 |
| **Synthesis quality** | Integrates 3 source types into coherent argument | Mostly integrates | Partly integrates | Cherry-picks one | × 2 |
| **Avoidance of false dichotomy** | Holds "yoga is valuable" AND "history is what it is" | Mostly nuanced | Hedges | Reduces to either-or | × 1 |
| **Prose / structure** | Polished | Clear | Some issues | Hard to follow | × 1 |

Max raw: 60. Paper = (sum × weights) × 65/60.

## Format deductions

- 5–6 pages (deduct 5 outside)
- Title page + thesis (deduct 5 missing)
- Works-cited (deduct 5 missing/wrong)
- IAST diacritics correct (deduct 3)

## Safety violation in project recommendations

- If a paper recommends an unsafe practice without contraindication note, deduct 5 from "Conceptual accuracy / safety."
