# Two facts to start

1. The intellectual traditions IKS refers to are **millennia old**.
2. The label "IKS" as a unified category is **post-2000**, codified by NEP 2020 §4.6.

Both facts are real. Both matter.

# Primary source: Ṛg Veda 1.1

> *agnim īḷe purohitaṁ yajñasya devam ṛtvijam*

"I praise Agni, the household priest, the divine officiant of the sacrifice..."

The earliest layer of Indian intellectual life — ritual hymns.

---

# Primary source: Īśa Upaniṣad

> *īśā vāsyam idaṁ sarvaṁ yat kiñca jagatyāṁ jagat*

"Whatever moves in this moving world is enveloped by the Lord."

The later layer — abstract metaphysical reflection.

**The Veda contains both. "Vedic" is a span, not a single voice.**

---

# Indian Mathematics — specifics

- **Bakhshali manuscript** (~7th c. CE) — earliest zero-as-number.
- **Bhāskara II** (1150 CE) — *Līlāvatī*, solved Pell's equation 500 yrs before Pell.
- **Mādhava** (~14th c. CE) — infinite series for π, 300 years before Leibniz.

---

# Indian Astronomy — specifics

- **Aryabhata** (~500 CE) — earth rotates on axis; π to 4 decimals.
- **Sūrya-siddhānta** — planetary calculations; earth's circumference ~1% accurate.
- **Varāhamihira** — compared 5 systems including Greek-derived.

---

# The Yavana exchange

Indo-Greek kingdoms (~2nd c. BCE – 1st c. CE) created sustained scholarly exchange.

Indian astronomers ACTIVELY engaged Greek astronomical work.

"Pure Indian" framings don't survive contact with the evidence.

**Synthesis is also an achievement.**

---

# The Madhava-Leibniz question

Mādhava had the π series ~300 years before Leibniz.

Why is it still called "Leibniz series" in most textbooks?

- European publication pipeline dominated.
- Kerala school's work not translated to European languages until late.
- Modern correction is in progress.

This is what real intellectual history looks like.
