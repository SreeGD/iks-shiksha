## Pacing notes

- Days 1–5 are math-and-procedure heavy. Days 6–10 are reading and writing heavy.
- The Dani 1993 reading on Day 6 is the most challenging; budget 20+ minutes for it.
- Project tracks (math vs history) split the class — plan supports for both during Days 8–9.

## What students leave with

- Sub-60-second 4×4 column addition by dot method.
- Working knowledge of all 16 Tirthaji sūtras.
- Ability to extend the dot method to non-decimal bases.
- An informed position on the historicity of "Vedic mathematics" claims.
- One research paper.
