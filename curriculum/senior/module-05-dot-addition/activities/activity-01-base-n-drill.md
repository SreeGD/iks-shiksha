# Activity 01 — Base-n Dot Method Drill

**Module:** Bindu Paddhati (Senior) · **Day:** 5 · **Time:** 15 min within Day 5

## Goal

Students apply the dot method to columns in base 8 and base 16, verifying their answers by base-10 conversion.

## Materials

- Drill sheet (below)
- Base-conversion reference card

## Instructions

> Use the dot method. Mark dots. Compute units + carry. Verify by base-10 conversion.

### Drill (10 problems)

| # | Base | Column |
|---|------|--------|
| 1 | 8 | 5, 4, 7, 2 |
| 2 | 8 | 6, 3, 7, 5, 4 |
| 3 | 8 | 2, 7, 1, 6, 3 |
| 4 | 16 | 9, A, 5, 7 |
| 5 | 16 | F, 3, 8, A |
| 6 | 16 | 7, B, 4, C, 2 |
| 7 | 2 | 1, 1, 1, 0, 1, 1 |
| 8 | 2 | 1, 0, 1, 1, 1, 1, 0, 1 |
| 9 | 10 | 8, 7, 5, 6, 3, 4 (warm-up check) |
| 10 | 10 | 4, 9, 7, 2, 8, 1, 6 (warm-up check) |

### Answer key

| # | Units | Carry | Verify (base 10 sum) |
|---|-------|-------|------------------------|
| 1 | 2 (in 8) | 2 | 18 = 22_(8) ✓ |
| 2 | 7 (in 8) | 3 | 25 = 31_(8) ✓ |
| 3 | 7 (in 8) | 2 | 19 = 23_(8) ✓ |
| 4 | F (in 16) | 1 | 31 = 1F_(16) ✓ |
| 5 | 4 (in 16) | 2 | 36 = 24_(16) ✓ |
| 6 | A (in 16) | 2 | 42 = 2A_(16) ✓ |
| 7 | 1 (in 2) | 10 | 5 = 101_(2). Units=1, carries=2 (= 10_(2)) |
| 8 | 0 (in 2) | 110 | 6 = 110_(2). Units=0, carries=3 (= 11_(2)) |
| 9 | 3 | 3 | 33 ✓ |
| 10 | 7 | 3 | 37 ✓ |

(Binary problems are tricky — students may need help with the "carry as binary number" interpretation.)

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Try the binary problems carefully.
- **Tier down:** Stick to base 8.

## Clean-up

- Sheets collected for spot-check.
