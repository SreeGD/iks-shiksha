# Paper Rubric — Module 5 (Senior)

**Paper marks:** 65 (out of 100 with summative 35).

## Math track criteria

| Criterion | 4 | 3 | 2 | 1 | Weight |
|-----------|---|---|---|---|--------|
| **Thesis clarity** | Crystal-clear, specific | Clear | Vague | None | × 3 |
| **Procedural correctness** | All worked examples correct; algorithm precisely stated | Mostly correct | One error | Multiple errors | × 3 |
| **Mathematical rigour** | Proof or rigorous justification | Argument-form justification | Hand-waving | None | × 2 |
| **Use of sources** | ≥3 sources cited correctly (Tirthaji + 2 others) | 2 sources | 1 source | None | × 2 |
| **Originality** | Original generalisation OR comparison | Independent work | Mostly synthesis | Copied | × 1 |
| **Prose / structure** | Polished | Clear | Some issues | Hard to follow | × 1 |

## History track criteria

| Criterion | 4 | 3 | 2 | 1 | Weight |
|-----------|---|---|---|---|--------|
| **Thesis clarity** | Crystal-clear position | Clear | Vague | None | × 3 |
| **Steel-manned counter** | Strongest opposing view fairly stated + responded | Counter present + addressed | Acknowledged | Strawmanned or ignored | × 3 |
| **Source quality** | Tirthaji's preface + Dani 1993 + Plofker (or 3rd) | 2 of these | 1 of these | None | × 2 |

| **Originality** | Surprising synthesis | Independent thinking | Mostly synthesis | Copied | × 1 |
| **Prose / structure** | Polished | Clear | Some issues | Hard to follow | × 1 |

Max raw: 48. Paper score = (sum × weights) × 65/48.

## Format requirements (binary deductions)

- 5–6 pages (deduct 5 outside)
- Title page + thesis (deduct 5 missing)
- Works-cited (deduct 5 missing/wrong format)
- IAST diacritics correct where Sanskrit appears (deduct 3)
