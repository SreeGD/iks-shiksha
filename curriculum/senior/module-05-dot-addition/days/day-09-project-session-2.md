# Day 9 — Project Session 2: Draft + Counter

**Module:** Bindu Paddhati (Senior) · **Time:** 45 min

## Learning objective

Each student reaches at least 2 drafted pages including a counter-argument and response.

## Materials

- In-progress drafts

## Flow

### Warm-up (3 min)

- Brief: today's focus is the counter-argument.

### Drafting + buddy work (35 min)

**Sprint 1 (15 min) — Draft body.** Quiet writing.

**Sprint 2 (15 min) — Counter-argument workshop.**
- Buddy pair-up (match math + history when possible — fresh eyes).
- Read each other's drafts.
- Each pair gives the strongest counter to the other's thesis.
- Student notes the counter and decides response.

**Sprint 3 (5 min) — Incorporate.** Each student writes the counter-argument paragraph or sketches its placement.

### Wrap-up (7 min)

- 3 students share the best counter they discovered.
- Outline-to-date submitted.

## Homework

- Finish complete draft. Bring tomorrow.

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Two distinct counter-arguments — different angles.
- **Tier down:** Submit detailed outline + 1 paragraph if a full draft isn't ready.

## Teacher notes

- Cross-track buddy pairing (math + history) often yields strong counters because the perspectives differ.
