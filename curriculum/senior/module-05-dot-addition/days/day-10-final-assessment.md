# Day 10 — Summative + Paper Submission

**Module:** Bindu Paddhati (Senior) · **Time:** 45 min

## Learning objective

Students complete summative quiz and submit 5–6 page paper.

## Materials

- Summative quiz (`quizzes/summative.md`)
- Stopwatch

## Flow

### Quiz (35 min)

- 35 marks. MC, sūtra identification, dot method + base-*n*, sūtra-application, short answer on historicity, open response.

### Paper submission (10 min)

- Submit printed paper or upload.
- Teacher quick-checks: title page + thesis, page count, works-cited.

### Wrap-up

- Reflection slip.

## Homework

- None. Module complete.

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Students who finish early start the next module's prerequisite reading.
- **Tier down:** Open-notebook quiz for documented learning needs.

## Teacher notes

- Mark papers within 5 school days.
- Math-track papers: check the proof for correctness.
- History-track papers: check that Dani is steel-manned, not strawmanned.
