# Module 5 (Senior) — Formative Quizzes

## Part A — Day 4 (10 min, 5 questions)

**1.** (1 mark) Which sūtra means "by addition and by subtraction"?
**2.** (1 mark) Which sūtra means "by completing or non-completing"?
**3.** (2 marks) Apply Sankalana: 234 + 98 = ? (show your sūtra-style step)
**4.** (2 marks) Apply Sankalana: 145 − 99 = ?
**5.** (2 marks) Compute 99 × 4 using Ekanyūnena Pūrveṇa.

### Answer key

| Q | Answer | Marks |
|---|--------|-------|
| 1 | Sankalana Vyavakalanābhyām (sūtra 7) | 1 |
| 2 | Pūraṇāpūraṇābhyām (sūtra 8) | 1 |
| 3 | 234 + 100 − 2 = 332 | 2 |
| 4 | 145 − 100 + 1 = 46 | 2 |
| 5 | 99 × 4 = (100 − 1) × 4 = 400 − 4 = 396 | 2 |

Total: 8 marks.

## Part B — Day 5 (10 min, 5 questions on base-n)

**1.** (1 mark) Convert 25 (base 10) to base 8.
**2.** (1 mark) Convert 1A (base 16) to base 10.
**3.** (2 marks) Apply the dot method in base 8 to column: 4, 7, 3, 6, 5. Units? Carry?
**4.** (2 marks) Apply the dot method in base 16 to column: 8, A, 5, 7. Units (in hex)? Carry?
**5.** (2 marks) Explain in one sentence why the dot method works in ANY base.

### Answer key

| Q | Answer | Marks |
|---|--------|-------|
| 1 | 31_(8) | 1 |
| 2 | 26 | 1 |
| 3 | 4+7+3+6+5 = 25_(10) = 31_(8). Units=1, carry=3. | 2 |
| 4 | 8+A+5+7 = 8+10+5+7 = 30_(10) = 1E_(16). Units=E, carry=1. | 2 |
| 5 | A dot represents "the base used up" = 1 carried to the next column. The carry rule is the same in any positional base. | 2 |

Total: 8 marks.
