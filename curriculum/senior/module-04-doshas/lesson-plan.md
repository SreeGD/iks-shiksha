# Lesson Plan — Doshas — Vāta, Pitta, Kapha (Senior)

10-day arc, 45 min per session.

### Day 1 — Three Doshas Introduction

**Objective:** Name vāta, pitta, kapha and recognise one observable trait of each.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (20 min) · Activity (15 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 2 — Vāta — The Motion Principle

**Objective:** Identify vāta-style behaviours (quick, dry, cold, moving) in self and surroundings.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (20 min) · Activity (15 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 3 — Pitta — The Transformation Principle

**Objective:** Identify pitta-style behaviours (warm, sharp, focused, transforming).

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (20 min) · Activity (15 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 4 — Kapha — The Structure Principle

**Objective:** Identify kapha-style behaviours (steady, cool, moist, structured).

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (20 min) · Activity (15 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 5 — Mapping to the Five Elements

**Objective:** Connect each doṣa to its two constituent elements (vāta=ākāśa+vāyu, pitta=tejas+ap, kapha=ap+pṛthvī).

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (20 min) · Activity (15 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 6 — Prakṛti Self-Assessment

**Objective:** Use a worksheet to estimate own dominant doṣa(s) — emphasising NOT diagnosis.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (20 min) · Activity (15 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 7 — Doṣa Rhythms in a Day

**Objective:** Map the kapha-pitta-vāta cycle across morning/noon/evening hours.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (20 min) · Activity (15 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 8 — Doṣa Rhythms Across Seasons

**Objective:** Map doṣa cycles to monsoon/summer/winter.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (20 min) · Activity (15 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 9 — Balance and Imbalance

**Objective:** Distinguish prakṛti (innate) from vikṛti (current state); recognise simple imbalance signs.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (20 min) · Activity (15 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 10 — Synthesis and Project Pitch

**Objective:** Pitch the 7-day self-observation project and answer assessment quiz.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (20 min) · Activity (15 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

## Pacing notes

- Senior sessions are 45 min. Adjust if your block is different.
- Days 5 and 9 are buffer days — use to reinforce weaker concepts or extend stronger ones.
- The summative project (see `assessments/project-brief.md`) runs in parallel from Day 6 onward.

## Differentiation

- **For advanced students:** see `teacher-notes.md` for extension prompts.
- **For students needing more support:** simplify activities, do more pair-work, allow oral instead of written responses.
