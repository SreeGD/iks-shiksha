# Lesson Plan — Moon Phases and Tithi (Senior)

10-day arc, 45 min per session.

### Day 1 — The Moon and Its Shapes

**Objective:** Observe and name the four basic phases (new, first quarter, full, last quarter).

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (20 min) · Activity (15 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 2 — Two Fortnights — Pakṣa

**Objective:** Distinguish śukla pakṣa from kṛṣṇa pakṣa and name today's.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (20 min) · Activity (15 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 3 — Fifteen Tithis per Pakṣa

**Objective:** Recite the 15 tithi names (pratipadā, dvitīyā, ...) and explain why a tithi isn't exactly a day.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (20 min) · Activity (15 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 4 — Building a Tithi Calendar

**Objective:** Construct a tithi calendar for the current month using observation and computation.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (20 min) · Activity (15 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 5 — Lunar Months and Festivals

**Objective:** Map the 12 lunar months (caitra, vaiśākha, ...) to major festivals.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (20 min) · Activity (15 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 6 — Panchanga — Five Limbs

**Objective:** Identify the five components: tithi, vāra, nakṣatra, yoga, karaṇa.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (20 min) · Activity (15 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 7 — Why Eclipses Happen

**Objective:** Explain solar/lunar eclipses using orbital plane geometry (5° tilt of Moon's orbit).

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (20 min) · Activity (15 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 8 — Modern Lunar Science

**Objective:** Phases as Sun's illumination of Moon; mention libration, tides.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (20 min) · Activity (15 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 9 — Cross-cultural Calendars

**Objective:** Compare with Hijri (Islamic), Chinese, and Hebrew lunar calendars.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (20 min) · Activity (15 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 10 — Synthesis and Quiz

**Objective:** Final quiz and 10-day moon-diary share.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (20 min) · Activity (15 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

## Pacing notes

- Senior sessions are 45 min. Adjust if your block is different.
- Days 5 and 9 are buffer days — use to reinforce weaker concepts or extend stronger ones.
- The summative project (see `assessments/project-brief.md`) runs in parallel from Day 6 onward.

## Differentiation

- **For advanced students:** see `teacher-notes.md` for extension prompts.
- **For students needing more support:** simplify activities, do more pair-work, allow oral instead of written responses.
