## Section A — Multiple choice (5 × 1 = 5 marks)

**1.** Classical Sāṅkhya enumerates how many tattvas?
a) 5  b) 8  c) 25  d) 108

**2.** The tanmātra of *tejas* (fire) is:
a) śabda  b) sparśa  c) rūpa  d) gandha

**3.** Empedocles's four "roots" are:
a) earth, water, fire, air  b) earth, water, fire, ether
c) sattva, rajas, tamas, ākāśa  d) hot, cold, wet, dry

a) the substance of perception
b) the substance of the celestial spheres
c) the same as Sāṅkhya's ākāśa

## Section E — Argumentative response (12 marks)

**11.** In 1–2 pages, argue your position on the following question:

> "Modern science has rendered the Sāṅkhya five-element framework obsolete except as cultural history. Discuss."

Your answer should:
- State a clear position (agree, disagree, partially).
- Cite at least 2 sources (primary scriptural, secondary scholarly, comparative, or modern science).
- Anticipate and respond to one counter-argument.
- Avoid the false dichotomy ("either Sāṅkhya is correct OR science is correct").

Use the back of this sheet and an additional sheet if needed.
