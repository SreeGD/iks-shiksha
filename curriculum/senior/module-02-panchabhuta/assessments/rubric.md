# Paper Rubric — Module 2 Pañcabhūta (Senior)

**Paper marks:** 65 (out of 100 with summative quiz 35).

## Criteria

| Criterion | 4 — Distinction | 3 — Merit | 2 — Pass | 1 — Resit | Weight |
|-----------|-------------------|-----------|----------|-------------|--------|
| **Thesis clarity** | Crystal-clear, specific, defensible thesis stated in opening | Clear thesis | Thesis present but vague | No clear thesis | × 3 |
| **Use of primary scripture** | At least 1 verse quoted in IAST + translation; correctly interpreted | Cites a verse; interpretation adequate | Mentions scripture vaguely | No primary scripture | × 2 |
| **Use of secondary scholarship** | Cites at least 1 academic source (Larson, Plofker, Bryant); used substantively | Cites a scholarly source | Vague academic reference | None | × 2 |
| **Cross-cultural / modern comparison** | Strong comparison with at least 1 specific point | Comparison present | Surface-level comparison | None | × 2 |
| **Counter-argument** | Steel-manned opposing view + reasoned response | Counter-argument present | Acknowledged opposing view | Ignored | × 2 |
| **Avoidance of false dichotomy** | Refuses "either-or"; holds multiple frameworks | Mostly nuanced | Some both-and reasoning | Reduces to either-or | × 1 |
| **Originality** | Original synthesis; surprising insight | Independent thinking visible | Mostly synthesises others | Largely paraphrased | × 1 |
| **Prose / structure** | Polished, well-paragraphed, clean citations | Clear, mostly clean | Some clarity issues | Hard to follow | × 1 |

Max raw: 56. Paper score = (sum × weights) × 65/56.

## Format requirements (binary, deduct only)

- 5 pages ± 1 (deduct 5 marks if outside)
- Title page + thesis (deduct 5 if missing)
- Works-cited with proper format (deduct 5 if missing or formatting wrong)
- IAST diacritics used correctly where Sanskrit appears (deduct 3 if absent)

## Late submission

- -5 marks per school day, capped at -20.

## Plagiarism

- A paper that closely matches an online source without citation goes to 0 on Originality AND on the source criteria.
- Single-day resubmission allowed once.
