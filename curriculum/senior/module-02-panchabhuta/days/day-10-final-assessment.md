# Day 10 — Summative Quiz + Paper Submission

**Module:** Pañcabhūta (Senior) · **Time:** 45 min (paper accepted up to end of day)

## Learning objective

Students complete the summative quiz and submit their 5-page argumentative paper.

## Materials

- Summative quiz (`quizzes/summative.md`)
- Stopwatch

## Flow

### Quiz (35 min)

- Distribute. Closed-book.
- 35 marks. Multiple choice, short answer, primary-source reading question, comparison question, open response.

### Paper submission (10 min)

- Hand in printed papers (or upload digitally).
- Teacher quick-checks each for:
  - Title page with name + thesis
  - Page count (4.5–5.5 pages target)
  - Works-cited page with at least 3 sources
- Any paper missing a piece is flagged for late penalty.

### Wrap-up

- Brief class closure.
- Reflection slip submitted (3 questions from `assessments/project-brief.md`).

## Homework

- None. Module complete. (Paper marked within 1 week.)

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Students who submit early can begin reading for the next module's prerequisites.
- **Tier down:** Open-notebook quiz for documented learning needs.

## Teacher notes

- Mark papers in two passes: first pass for clarity and structure; second pass for source quality and counter-argument fairness.
- Return marked papers within 5 school days with detailed comments.

## Citation(s) used

- All sources verified.
