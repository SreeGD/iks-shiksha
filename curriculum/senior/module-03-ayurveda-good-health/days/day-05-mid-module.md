# Day 5 — Mid-Module + Paper Topic

**Module:** Ayurveda (Senior) · **Time:** 45 min

## Flow

### Quiz (20 min)

Formative Part B, 12 marks.

### Topic commitment (20 min)

Each student writes:
- Working title
- One-paragraph thesis (specific Ayurvedic claim being examined)
- 3 sources: ≥1 classical primary, ≥1 modern empirical, ≥1 historical/scholarly

Suggested topics:
- "Curcumin's anti-inflammatory action — what does the modern evidence say?"
- "The Tridosha typology: clinical heuristic or biomedical classification?"
- "Dinacharya and circadian biology — convergent or coincidental?"
- "Should AYUSH practitioners be allowed to prescribe biomedical drugs?"
- "Sushruta's surgical legacy — what specifically did European surgeons learn?"

### Wrap-up (5 min)

- Topic + thesis submitted at door.
