## Pacing

- Day 2 (Caraka reading) needs quiet time.
- Day 4 (modern evidence) requires students to engage RCT abstracts — non-trivial.
- Day 7 debate is loaded; honour the discomfort.

## What students leave with

- Ayurveda mapped within IKS at scholarly depth.
- Primary source reading skill (Caraka in IAST).
- A defensible position on Ayurveda-in-modern-healthcare.
- One research paper integrating classical + empirical + historical sources.
