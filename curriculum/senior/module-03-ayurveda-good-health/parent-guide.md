# Parent Guide — Module 3 (Senior): Ayurveda

**Your student's age:** Senior (Grades 9–12) · **Module length:** 10 sessions × 45 min

## What your student is learning

A serious comparative-medicine module:

- Ayurveda's place within IKS — Upaveda of the Atharva Veda, 8 branches (*aṣṭāṅga*), 3 foundational texts (Caraka, Suśruta, Aṣṭāṅga Hṛdaya).
- **Primary-source reading** — Caraka Saṃhitā in IAST + translation.
- **Tridosha theory in depth** — classical exposition + contemporary scholarly engagement.
- **Modern evidence base** — what survives RCT scrutiny (turmeric anti-inflammatory, dinacharya / circadian biology, yoga / HRV), what doesn't (dosha typology as strict biomarker classification).
- **The integration debate** — AYUSH ministry, AIIMS integration units, NHS pilots, professional medical body positions.
- **A 5–6 page research-synthesis paper** integrating classical primary, modern empirical, and historical/scholarly sources.

## The honest framing

- Ayurveda is a sophisticated, real framework with substantial evidence base for parts of it.
- It is NOT a substitute for modern medicine where modern medicine works (infections, emergencies, surgery, mental health crises).
- The "either-or" framing is false. The "both-and" framing requires honest discrimination between well-evidenced and weakly-evidenced claims.

## Important safety note

**Nothing in this module is medical advice.** If your student is on any medication or treatment, that continues unchanged. If they propose stopping treatment because of this module, intervene.

## How to help

**Helpful:**
- Discuss family Ayurvedic traditions honestly — what works, what's habit, what's untested.
- For the paper: be a thoughtful skeptic. "Where's the evidence?" is the key question.
- If you're in healthcare yourself, share your professional experience honestly.

**Not helpful:**
- Push them toward "Ayurveda is all true" or "Ayurveda is all false." Neither serves.
- Discourage engagement with the integration debate. It's politically loaded; learning to engage politically loaded scholarly questions is a real skill.

## Assessment

- Summative quiz: 35
- Paper: 65
- Total: 100

## What success looks like

- Your student can read a Caraka Saṃhitā verse in IAST with translation.
- They can articulate where Ayurveda has strong evidence and where it doesn't.
- They can steel-man all three integration positions.
- They write a research paper that integrates 3 source types fairly.
