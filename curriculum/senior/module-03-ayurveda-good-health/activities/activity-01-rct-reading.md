# Activity 01 — Read an RCT Abstract

**Module:** Ayurveda (Senior) · **Day:** 4 · **Time:** 15 min within Day 4

## Goal

Students read a real peer-reviewed abstract on an Ayurvedic intervention and critique it.

## Materials

- Printed abstract: Aggarwal & Harikumar (2009) on curcumin (or substitute another)

## Instructions

Read silently. Then answer in your workbook:

1. What's the intervention?
2. What conditions does the abstract cover?
3. What strength of evidence does it claim?
4. What hedge words appear? ("may," "possibly," "limited data," "further research needed")
5. What would a strong skeptic critique?

## Discussion (5 min)

Pair share answers.

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Find a related full RCT via Google Scholar; critique sample size, controls, blinding.
- **Tier down:** Focus on Qs 1–3.
