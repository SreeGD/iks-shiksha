# Module 9 (Middle) — Formative Quizzes

Two parts: Part A on Day 3, Part B on Day 5.

---

## Part A — Day 3 (5 min, 5 short problems)

**Name:** ______________________  **Class:** ______

Find the complement of each number to the next power of ten. Use the Nikhilam rule: all from 9, the last from 10.

**1.** Complement of **47** to 100: ______

**2.** Complement of **283** to 1000: ______

**3.** Complement of **904** to 1000: ______

**4.** Complement of **3 752** to 10 000: ______

**5.** State the Nikhilam sūtra (in English is fine): ______________________________________________

### Part A answer key

| Q | Answer | Common error |
|---|--------|--------------|
| 1 | 53 (9−4=5, 10−7=3) | Mixing 9 and 10 columns |
| 2 | 717 (9−2, 9−8, 10−3) | Last digit "from 9" instead of "from 10" |
| 3 | 96 — i.e. 096 (9−9, 9−0, 10−4) | Forgetting the leading zero |
| 4 | 6 248 (9−3, 9−7, 9−5, 10−2) | Off-by-one on a digit |
| 5 | "All from nine and the last from ten" (or the Sanskrit *Nikhilaṁ Navataścaramaṁ Daśataḥ*) | Vague paraphrase |

## Part B — Day 5 (12 min, 12 mixed problems)

**Name:** ______________________  **Class:** ______

**Section 1 — Complement recall (3 marks)**

  1a. Complement of **62** to 100: ______
  1b. Complement of **148** to 1000: ______
  1c. Complement of **4 591** to 10 000: ______

**Section 2 — Power-of-ten subtractions (4 marks)**

  2a. `1000 − 358 =` ______
  2b. `10 000 − 6 427 =` ______
  2c. `1000 − 72 =` ______   (Hint: pad with leading zero.)
  2d. `100 000 − 12 345 =` ______

**Section 3 — Padding cases (3 marks)**

  3a. `1000 − 9 =` ______
  3b. `10 000 − 87 =` ______
  3c. `1000 − 999 =` ______

**Section 4 — Short answer (2 marks)**

  **4.** Why does the LAST digit take "from 10" instead of "from 9"? Answer in one sentence.
  ___________________________________________________________

**Total Part B:** 12 marks.

## What to do with the results

- Class average below 7/12 → reteach Day 6 on "why it works" before moving to Day 7. Reinforce padding with quick warm-up drills.
- Individual below 4/12 → pull aside on Day 7 for 10-min reteaching of complements + padding.
- Section 3 (padding) is the highest-error section in field-test data. Don't be surprised if it pulls scores down. Address explicitly.
