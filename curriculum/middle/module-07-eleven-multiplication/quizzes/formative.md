## What to do with the results

- Class average below 8/14 = reteach Day 3 (the algebra) and Day 4 (3-digit). Combine Day 6 with a re-drill.
- Individual below 5/14 = pull aside on Day 6 for 10-min reteaching of the carry case.
- Class average above 12/14 = proceed to *Anurūpyeṇa* (Day 6) at planned pace; consider tier-up problems for Day 9.
