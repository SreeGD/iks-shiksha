# Writing Prompts — Module 3 (Middle)

1. **(Day 1)** What was your impression of Ayurveda before this module? In 5 sentences, what changed?
2. **(Day 3)** After the self-assessment — do you agree with the result? Why or why not?
3. **(Day 4)** Pick one dinacharya practice. Describe what trying it for one day felt like.
4. **(Day 6)** Pick one of the 6 tastes. Describe a meal that's heavy in this taste and what dosha effect it would have.
5. **(Day 7)** BG 6.17 names 5 activities to regulate. Pick the one you find HARDEST. In 5 sentences, explain why.
6. **(Day 10)** Argue: should Ayurveda be taught in school PE / health class? Defend your position in 1 page.
7. **(Cross-disciplinary)** Modern circadian biology says daily rhythms matter for health. Dinacharya says the same. Are they the SAME insight or DIFFERENT? Defend in 1 paragraph.
