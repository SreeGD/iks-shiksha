## Pacing

- Day 3 self-assessment is FOR FUN, not diagnostic. State this firmly.
- Day 4 dinacharya is the most practical session — protect time.
- Day 10 needs ~4 min per student presenting their dinacharya.

## Daily warm-up

Each class opens with: *"Which dosha did you notice most yesterday?"* (mood/energy/food/sleep observation). 2 students share.

## What students leave with

- Vocabulary: Ayurveda, Swasthya, dosha, dinacharya.
- The Tridosha framework with examples.
- A personal 7-day dinacharya.
- BG 6.17 in IAST + translation.
- The discipline of distinguishing lifestyle medicine from acute medicine.
