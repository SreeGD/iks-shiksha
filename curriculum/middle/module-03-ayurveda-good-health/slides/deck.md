# What is Ayurveda?

*āyu* (life) + *veda* (knowledge) = **science of life**.

2000+ years old. A framework for daily wellbeing.

---

# Swasthya

*swa* (self) + *stha* (established) = **wellbeing**.

Physical + mental + social balance.

---

# What Ayurveda covers — and doesn't

**Covers well:** lifestyle, prevention, daily routine, food, herbal medicine.

**Doesn't cover well:** acute infections, emergency surgery, modern diagnostics.

**Honest framing:** Ayurveda + modern medicine = partners, not rivals.

# The Tridosha

| Dosha | Elements | Function |
|-------|----------|----------|
| **Vāta** | air + space | movement, breath |
| **Pitta** | fire + water | digestion, metabolism |
| **Kapha** | water + earth | structure, immunity |

**Everyone has all three.**

---

# Prakṛti vs Vikṛti

**Prakṛti** = your inborn constitution.

**Vikṛti** = your current state (can be out of balance).

The aim of Ayurveda: keep vikṛti close to prakṛti.

# Dinacharya — the daily routine

| Time | Practice |
|------|----------|
| ~Sunrise | Wake + water |
| Morning | Oral hygiene, oil massage |
| Mid-morning | Exercise + largest meal |
| Afternoon | Focused work |
| Evening | Light dinner before sunset |
| ~10pm | Sleep |

---

# The 24-hour dosha clock

```
06–10am: Kapha (heavy, slow)
10am–2pm: Pitta (digestive fire peak)
02–06pm: Vāta (active, mobile)
06–10pm: Kapha
10pm–02am: Pitta (cellular repair)
02–06am: Vāta
```

Plan activities to match the dosha block.

# Modern science overlap

- **Circadian biology** — Nobel 2017 (Hall, Rosbash, Young).
- **Time-restricted eating** — Satchidananda Panda.
- **Sleep science** — confirms 10pm-ish optimal.
- **Oil massage** — emerging evidence on skin / lymphatics.

Tradition observed centuries before modern measurement.

# Ahāra — six tastes

| Sanskrit | English | Example |
|----------|---------|---------|
| Madhura | Sweet | Rice, milk |
| Amla | Sour | Yogurt, lemon |
| Lavaṇa | Salty | Salt |
| Kaṭu | Pungent | Chili, ginger |
| Tikta | Bitter | Bitter gourd, neem |
| Kaṣāya | Astringent | Banana, pomegranate |

A balanced meal = ALL six tastes.

# Primary source — BG 6.17

> *yuktāhāra-vihārasya yukta-ceṣṭasya karmasu*
> *yukta-svapnāvabodhasya yogo bhavati duḥkha-hā*

"He who is regulated in eating, recreation, work, sleep, and waking — for him, yoga becomes the destroyer of sorrow."

— Bhagavad-gītā 6.17

---

# Five activities to regulate

1. *Āhāra* — eating
2. *Vihāra* — recreation
3. *Ceṣṭā* in *karma* — effort in work
4. *Svapna* — sleep
5. *Avabodha* — waking
