**Project marks:** 20 (+ summative 20 → 40 total).

| Criterion | 4 | 3 | 2 | 1 | Weight |
|-----------|---|---|---|---|--------|
| Practical realism | Achievable; thought through for actual school week | Mostly realistic | Some unrealistic items | Unrealistic | × 2 |
| Ayurvedic principle | Every item has a correct principle | Most items principled | Some items vague | Items unjustified | × 1.5 |
| BG 6.17 connection | One item explicitly linked to BG 6.17 activity | Connection present | Vague mention | Missing | × 1 |

| Communication | Confident, audible, within time | Mostly clear | Some clarity issues | Hard to follow | × 1 |

Max raw: 26. Project score = (sum × weights) × 20/26.

## Common deductions

- Recommending changes that medically risk a child with a known condition: −5 and intervention.
- "Wake at 4am" without realism note: −2.
- No BG 6.17 connection: −2.
