# Project Brief — Module 3 (Middle)

**Type:** Personal 7-day dinacharya + 3-min presentation · **Marks:** 20

## What you'll do

Design a 7-day dinacharya for yourself. For each practice, write one line explaining the Ayurvedic principle. Then present it to the class in 3 minutes.

## Required elements

1. **Weekday routine** — wake, morning, midday, evening, sleep.
2. **Weekend variation** — what's different on Sat/Sun.
3. **Ayurvedic principle** per item — why the tradition recommends it.
4. **Realism statement** — confirm this routine is actually achievable.
5. **One BG 6.17 citation** — connect one item to one of the five activities Krishna names.

## Format

- 1-page worksheet (template provided).
- 3-min spoken presentation on Day 10.

## Rubric (see `rubric.md`)

Practical realism (×2) · Ayurvedic principle accuracy (×1.5) · BG 6.17 connection (×1) · Personal honesty (×1) · Communication (×1).

## Self-reflection (Day 10)

In 4 sentences:
1. What did you commit to changing?
2. What did you discover about your current routine?
3. What practice will you keep beyond this module?
