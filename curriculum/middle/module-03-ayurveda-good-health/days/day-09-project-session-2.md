# Day 9 — Project Session 2: Test + Refine

**Module:** Ayurveda (Middle) · **Time:** 45 min

## Learning objective

Each student refines their dinacharya based on yesterday's trial and prepares a 3-min presentation.

## Materials

- In-progress dinacharya drafts

## Flow

### Warm-up (5 min)

- Quick share: which dinacharya item went well last night? Which didn't?

### Refine (15 min)

- Adjust the dinacharya based on yesterday's experience.
- Add a one-line justification per item (cite the principle).

### Buddy rehearse (15 min)

Pairs.
- 3-min presentation each: "My dinacharya — what, why, how I'll know it's working."
- 2-min feedback.

### Wrap-up (10 min)

- 3 students share one change they made.
- Final touches at home.

## Homework

- Finalise dinacharya. Practise presentation once.
