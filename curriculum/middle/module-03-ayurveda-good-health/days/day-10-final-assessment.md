# Day 10 — Final + Presentations

**Module:** Ayurveda (Middle) · **Time:** 60 min (extend if possible)

## Learning objective

Students demonstrate mastery via summative quiz + 3-min dinacharya presentation.

## Flow

### Quiz (20 min)

20 marks. MC + short answer + BG 6.17 reading + open response.

### Presentations (35 min)

- 3 min talk + 1 min Q&A per student.
- Teacher marks rubric in real time.

### Wrap-up (5 min)

- Closing: "Pick the ONE dinacharya practice you'll keep beyond this module."
- Reflection slip.

## Homework

- None. Module done.
