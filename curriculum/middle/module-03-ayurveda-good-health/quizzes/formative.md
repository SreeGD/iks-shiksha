# Module 3 (Middle) — Formative Quizzes

## Part A — Day 3 (5 Q, 5 min)

1. (1) Define Āyurveda literally.
2. (1) Define Swasthya.
3. (1) Name the 3 doshas.
4. (1) Which elements make up Pitta?
5. (1) True or False: "Everyone has all three doshas." (T/F)

### Key
1. *āyu* (life) + *veda* (knowledge) = science of life.
2. *swa* (self) + *stha* (established) = wellbeing.
3. Vāta, Pitta, Kapha.
4. Fire + water.
5. T.

## Part B — Day 5 (8 Q, 12 min)

1. (1) Vāta is built from which elements?
2. (1) Kapha is built from which elements?
3. (2) Name 4 of the 6 tastes (rasa) in Ayurveda.
4. (1) Brāhma muhūrta refers to what?
5. (1) Pitta is dominant during which hours roughly?
6. (1) Why is the largest meal recommended at midday?
7. (1) What is dinacharya?
8. (2) Short answer: distinguish prakṛti from vikṛti.

### Key
1. Air + space. 2. Water + earth. 3. Any 4 of madhura, amla, lavaṇa, kaṭu, tikta, kaṣāya. 4. ~96 min before sunrise; the traditionally-recommended waking time. 5. 10am–2pm. 6. Digestive fire (Pitta) is at peak then. 7. Daily routine. 8. Prakṛti = the dosha constitution you're born with. Vikṛti = your current dosha state (which can be out of balance).
