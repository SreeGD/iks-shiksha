# Rubric — Module 1 (Middle) Project

**Project marks:** 20 (+ summative 20 → 40 total).

| Criterion | 4 | 3 | 2 | 1 | Weight |
|-----------|---|---|---|---|--------|
| Conceptual accuracy | Named practitioner + date + specific claim, all correct | Mostly correct | One factual error | Multiple errors | × 2 |
| Source quality | ≥1 primary OR scholarly source cited correctly | Source cited | Vague reference | None | × 1.5 |
| Specific claim | "X did Y in year Z, here's the evidence" | Specific but missing one element | Generic claim with one specific | Vague throughout | × 1.5 |
| Honest limitation | Names what the tradition did NOT do; resists triumphalism | Mentions a limit | Briefly mentions | Triumphalist or dismissive | × 1 |
| Communication | Clear, audible, within time | Mostly clear | Some clarity issues | Hard to follow | × 1 |

Max raw: 28. Project score = (sum × weights) × 20/28.

## Common deductions

- Triumphalism without limit-stating: −2 on Honest limitation.
- Source cited but not actually engaged: −2 on Source quality.
- Outside 4:30–5:30 timing: −1 on Communication.
