# Writing Prompts — Module 1 (Middle)

1. **Reflection (Day 1).** Before this module, what did you think "Indian Knowledge" meant? In 4–5 sentences, how has that changed?
2. **Reflection (Day 4).** Pick one Upaveda. Which problem in your own life could it speak to?
3. **Explanatory (Day 6, ~150 words).** Explain to a friend who has never heard of it: what is the Bakhshali manuscript, and why does it matter?
4. **Explanatory (Day 7, ~150 words).** What did Aryabhata get right? What did he NOT propose (despite the popular claim)?
5. **Argumentative (Day 5 or homework).** Some scholars argue "IKS" is too broad a category to be useful. Defend or refute in 1 page with one example.
6. **Creative (Day 4).** Write a 1-paragraph imaginary letter from Kauṭilya (the Arthaśāstra author) to a modern Indian Prime Minister.
7. **Cross-disciplinary.** Pick a contemporary issue (climate, public health, AI ethics). Which Upaveda's tradition has something to say? Defend.
