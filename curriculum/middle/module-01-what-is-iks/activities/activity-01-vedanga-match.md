# Activity 01 — Vedanga Matching Game

**Module:** What is IKS? (Middle) · **Day:** 3 · **Time:** 15 min

## Goal

In pairs, students match the 6 Vedangas (Sanskrit names) to their functions in a memory-card game.

## Materials per pair

- 12 cards: 6 with Sanskrit names, 6 with English function descriptions

### Cards (cut from printout)

**Names:** Śikṣā · Kalpa · Vyākaraṇa · Nirukta · Chandas · Jyotiṣa

**Functions:** Phonetics · Ritual procedure · Grammar · Etymology · Prosody · Astronomy & astrology

## Rules

1. Shuffle all 12 cards. Lay face-down in a 3×4 grid.
2. On your turn, flip 2 cards.
3. If they match (name + function), keep the pair.
4. If not, flip face-down. Other player goes.
5. Most pairs wins.

## Discussion (5 min after)

- Which match was hardest to remember?
- Why might "Jyotiṣa" combine astronomy AND astrology?

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Add 4 sub-cards with examples (e.g. "Pāṇini" matches "Vyākaraṇa").
- **Tier down:** Play with only 3 pairs (Vyākaraṇa, Jyotiṣa, Kalpa).
