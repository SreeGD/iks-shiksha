# Module 1 (Middle) — Formative Quizzes

## Part A — Day 3 (5 min, 5 Q)

1. (1) Name the 4 Vedas.
2. (1) Name the 4 layers within a Veda.
3. (1) Which Vedanga is grammar?
4. (1) Who wrote the *Aṣṭādhyāyī*?
5. (1) What is the difference between Vedanga and Upaveda?

### Key
1. Ṛg, Yajur, Sāma, Atharva. 2. Saṃhitā, Brāhmaṇa, Āraṇyaka, Upaniṣad. 3. Vyākaraṇa. 4. Pāṇini. 5. Vedangas = auxiliary disciplines for preserving Veda. Upavedas = applied sciences derived from Veda.
