# Day 9 — Project Session 2: Refine and Rehearse

**Module:** What is IKS? (Middle) · **Time:** 45 min

## Learning objective

Each student rehearses their 5-min presentation end-to-end with timed feedback from a buddy.

## Materials

- All in-progress materials
- Stopwatch

## Flow

### Warm-up (3 min)

Brief: today is refine + rehearse.

### Sprints (35 min)

**Sprint 1 (15 min) — Refine.** Teacher rotates with focused question: *"Show me your strongest 60 seconds."*

Common interventions:
- Missing the "honest limitation" — must include.
- Source cited but not actually engaged — push for a quote or paraphrase.
- Specific claim vague — sharpen.

**Sprint 2 (15 min) — Buddy rehearsal.** Pairs rehearse to each other. 5 min talk + 2 min feedback. Swap.

**Sprint 3 (5 min) — Adjust.** Each student decides one change.

### Wrap-up (7 min)

- 3 students share what they're changing.
- Final touches at home tonight.

## Homework

- Finalise. Bring everything tomorrow.

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Anticipate the toughest audience question and prepare a 30-sec answer.
- **Tier down:** Read from notes during presentation if needed.

## Teacher notes

- The buddy rehearsal is the most useful feedback. Encourage *kind and specific* feedback.
