# Day 10 — Final Presentations + Summative

**Module:** What is IKS? (Middle) · **Time:** 60 min (extend if possible)

## Learning objective

Students demonstrate mastery via 5-min presentations + short summative quiz.

## Materials

- Summative quiz (`quizzes/summative.md`)
- Rubric copy per student
- Stopwatch

## Flow

### Quiz (15 min)

20 marks: MC + short answer + matching + one open response.

### Presentations (40 min)

- 5 min talk + 1 min Q&A per student.
- ~6–7 students fit. Book a second period for the rest.
- During each:
  - Teacher marks rubric in real time.
  - 1 audience question per presentation.
  - "I learned / I wonder" slips for the presenter.

### Wrap-up (5 min)

- Closing question: "Pick the ONE Indian contribution you'll keep talking about beyond this class."
- Reflection slip.

## Homework

- None. Module complete.

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Early-finishers help peer-edit a struggling student's slide.
- **Tier down:** Open-notebook quiz for documented learning needs.

## Teacher notes

- Mark quizzes within 48 hours.
- Capture photos of best slides for the BACKLOG of "exemplars" for next year.
