# Day 5 — Mid-Module Check

**Module:** What is IKS? (Middle) · **Time:** 45 min

## Learning objective

Students consolidate Days 1–4 via a formative quiz and commit to a project topic.

## Materials

- Formative quiz Part B (`quizzes/formative.md`)
- Project brief handouts
- Topic sticky notes

## Flow

### Warm-up (5 min)

Daily prompt. Brief recap chorus: 4 Vedas, 4 layers, 6 Vedangas (just names).

### Formative quiz (15 min)

- `quizzes/formative.md` Part B.
- Closed notebook. 10 questions covering Vedas / layers / Vedangas / Upavedas / one named contribution.

### Review (10 min)

- Class walk-through of common errors. Don't grade now; mark tonight.

### Project commitment (10 min)

- Hand out project brief.
- Each student picks ONE strand:
  - Mathematics (zero, decimal place-value, infinite series, Bhāskara, Brahmagupta, Mādhava)
  - Astronomy (Aryabhata, Sūrya-siddhānta)
  - Medicine (Caraka, Suśruta, Āyurveda doshas)
  - Linguistics (Pāṇini's grammar)
  - Music (*Nāṭyaśāstra*, rāga system)
  - Architecture / Sthāpatya
  - Governance (Kauṭilya)
  - Other (with teacher approval)

- Write topic on a sticky note + post on the wall under their chosen domain.

### Wrap-up (5 min)

- Note: "Some domains will have 5+ students, some will have 1. Each will be different. That's fine."
- Preview Day 6: *"Tomorrow — deep on Indian mathematics. Zero. Place value. Infinite series."*

## Homework

- Submit project plan paragraph (working title + thesis sentence + 2 sources) by tomorrow.

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Pick a less common topic (Pāṇini, Mādhava). The research is harder; the project will stand out.
- **Tier down:** Pick a well-documented topic (zero, Aryabhata, Suśruta). Easier research.

## Teacher notes

- Mark the formative quiz tonight. Note any student who scored below 50% — schedule a 10-min check-in by Day 7.
- The project topic choice often surfaces what students are curious about. Adjust the depth of Days 6 (math) and 7 (astronomy) based on the distribution.

## Citation(s) used

- All sources covered to date.
