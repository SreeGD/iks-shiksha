# Activity 02 — Speed Sprint

**Module:** Nikhilam Multiplication · **Band:** Middle · **Day:** 9 · **Time:** 18 min within Day 9

## Type

Timed individual drill + paired chorus round.

## Goal

Each student solves 10 mixed-case Nikhilam problems mentally in 3 minutes; then pairs do a chorus-round of 10 more.

## Materials per pair

- 1 sprint sheet (10 problems).
- 1 pen each.
- 1 stopwatch (or use the wall clock).

## Instructions

> **Speed Sprint** — individual, 3 minutes per round.
>
> 1. **Round 1 (3 min):** Solve all 10 problems on sheet 1 as fast as possible. Mental, not paper-and-pencil if you can.
> 2. **Self-correct (1 min):** Use the answer key.
> 3. **Round 2 (3 min):** Solve all 10 on sheet 2.
> 4. **Self-correct (1 min).**
> 5. **Chorus round (5 min):** Pair up. Partner A reads problem; you both shout the answer. Switch. Cover 10 random problems.

## Scoring

- 10 correct in 3 min = "Exceeds" (sub-20-sec average).
- 7–9 correct = "Meets."
- 4–6 correct = "Approaches" — practise more before Day 10's quiz.
- 0–3 correct = "Beginning" — get a teacher 5-min reteach on Day 10.

## Going well / Needs intervention

| | Looks like | Sounds like |
|---|---|---|
| Going well | Quiet sustained focus, fast pencil-strokes, deficit-then-answer | (Nothing — they're solving) |
| Stuck | Confused face, restarting same problem 3 times | "Wait, is 88 above or below 100?" |

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Sprint round of 20 problems in 3 minutes; or include 4-digit problems near 10000.
- **Tier down:** 5 problems in 3 minutes (gentler pace); focus on accuracy over speed.

## Clean-up

- Sheet collected (for retest record).
- Pair-chorus round is unscored — just for chorus energy.
