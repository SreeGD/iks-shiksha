# Module 15 — Project Implementation — Capstone (Darśana)

**Sanskrit:** *Pañcagavya (and other capstones)* · **Band:** Middle (6-8) · **Duration:** 60 min · **Path:** Darśana (Workshop)

10-day applied project drawing on Modules 1-14.

> **Darśana note.** This workshop condenses Module 15's full 10-day arc into one hour. For the deeper version — daily lesson plans, quizzes, assessments, slide decks — switch to **[Adhyayana](../)** (the Full Course path).

---

## Opening hook · 5 min

Show the 10-option capstone menu. *"You've learned the frameworks. Today: pick ONE and apply it. The capstone is your work."*

---

## Core idea · 15 min

**Capstone menu (pick one):**

1. **Doṣa self-observation week** (Module 4) — track your sleep/food/mood for 7 days
2. **Herb identification deck** (Module 10) — 6 cards with Sanskrit, common name, active compound, use, safety
3. **Tithi/festival calendar** (Module 6) — current month's lunar calendar with festivals
4. **Sun-path tracker** (Module 12) — sunrise direction for 5 days, mapped on a year-circle
5. **Magic-square art** (Module 14) — design + decorate a 4 × 4 or 5 × 5
6. **Yoga sequence design** (Module 8) — 10-min sequence with limbs 1-4 mapped
7. **Sustainability audit of school** (Module 13) — water, waste, biodiversity
8. **Mental-math toolkit** (Modules 5, 7, 9, 11) — one-page reference card
9. **Pañcabhūta cuisine** (Module 2 + 3) — design a meal touching all 5 elements / 6 tastes
10. **Pañcagavya kitchen-science** — SAFE substitute: fermented foods (curd, paneer, batter) — NOT cow-urine ingestion.

**Safety floor:** food projects use familiar safe ingredients. Yoga avoids extreme poses. Outdoor projects require supervision.

---

## Demonstration · 10 min

Walk through one project end-to-end: e.g., "doṣa-rhythm clock" (Module 4) — scope → research → design → build → present. 10-min overview.

---

## Source moment · 5 min

Paraphrase Bhagavad Gītā 2.47: *"Your right is to action alone, never to its fruits."* (Modern translation: focus on the WORK, not the outcome.)

---

## Hands-on activity · 15 min

**Project scoping.** 30 minutes silent work: write a 1-page project plan with scope, sources, timeline (10 days), assessment criteria.

---

## Reflection + take-home · 10 min

*"Why is THIS project the right one for you? What will you learn that you couldn't learn in class?"*
