# Module 8 — Yoga and the Body

**Age band:** Middle · **Grades:** 6–8 · **Duration:** 10 sessions × 45 min (with ~15 min movement per session)

## Learning objectives

By the end of this module, students will be able to:

1. **Define** *yoga* using Patañjali's framing (*citta-vṛtti-nirodha* — settling the fluctuations of the mind) and Bhagavad-gītā's framing (yoga as skill in action).
2. **Recall** the eight limbs (*aṣṭāṅga*) of yoga and identify which two are physical (*āsana*, *prāṇāyāma*) versus mental.
3. **Perform** five foundational *āsanas* — *tāḍāsana, vṛkṣāsana, baddha-koṇāsana, balāsana, sukhāsana* — with safe alignment.
4. **Perform** the 12-pose *Sūrya Namaskāra* sequence with steady breath co-ordination.
5. **Practise** natural-breath observation (basic *prāṇāyāma*) and describe one observable effect on attention or calm.
6. **Connect** posture and breath to autonomic nervous system function (sympathetic vs. parasympathetic) — at a Middle-band level.

- Comfortable with basic stretching from PE class.
- No yoga experience assumed.
- Medical opt-out form completed if a student has any musculoskeletal or breathing condition (see safety notes in `teacher-notes.md`).

## Important safety note

This module involves physical practice. Read `teacher-notes.md` BEFORE Day 1. Inversions and advanced backbends are NOT taught at Middle band. Pranayama is limited to natural-breath observation only.
