# Day 9 — Project Session 2: Refine and Rehearse

**Module:** Yoga · **Band:** Middle · **Time:** 45 min

## Learning objective

By the end of this lesson, every pair will have rehearsed their 5-minute routine end-to-end and received feedback from a buddy pair.

## Materials

- All in-progress materials.
- Mats laid out.
- Stopwatch.
- Feedback slip per pair.

## Lesson flow

### Settle (2 min)

- Sukhāsana. 5 breaths.

### Core (30 min) — Two halves

**Half 1 (15 min) — Refine.**
- Each pair runs their routine once on their own mats, timed.
- Teacher rotates: 60 sec per pair, focused question: "Where does the energy of your routine drop?"
- Common adjustments to flag:
  - Pace too fast — students rush through poses.
  - Breath cues missing.
  - Transitions clunky.
  - Source citation forgotten — must be spoken aloud.

**Half 2 (15 min) — Buddy rehearsal.**
- Pair A demos routine to Pair B (mat-side). Pair B lies in the role of the target audience and tries to follow.
- 5 min run + 2 min feedback. Swap. Total ~14 min for round.

### Wrap-up (5 min)

- One-sentence share: "What's the one thing I'm changing before tomorrow?"
- Tomorrow: full presentations + summative quiz.

## Student-facing content

> **Project workshop — Session 2: Refine + Rehearse**
>
> Checklist:
> - [ ] My routine fits in 5 min ± 30 sec.
> - [ ] I cite at least one source aloud (during intro or close).
> - [ ] My breath cues are clear.
> - [ ] My partner and I know who leads which pose.
> - [ ] I've heard feedback from one buddy pair and decided what to change.
>
> Tomorrow you teach the class.

## Homework

- Practise the full routine one more time. Bring everything tomorrow.

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Add a memorable 1-line "takeaway" the audience will remember after the 5 min.
- **Tier down:** Reduce to 3 poses + 1 breath segment if 4–5 poses isn't sticking.

## Teacher notes

- The buddy-pair rehearsal is the most useful feedback step. Encourage feedback as *kind and specific* — "the transition from down-dog to lunge felt slow" beats "good job."
- Pairs who can't complete a clean run-through by end of class need extra support — invite a 15-min lunch-time rehearsal if possible.
