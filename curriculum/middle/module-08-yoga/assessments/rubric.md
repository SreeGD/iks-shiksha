# Project Rubric — Module 8 Yoga (Middle)

**Project marks:** 30 (out of 40 with presentation 10).

## Criteria

| Criterion | 4 — Exceeds | 3 — Meets | 2 — Approaches | 1 — Beginning | Weight |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|----------------|----------------|--------|
| **Āsana accuracy and safety** | Poses chosen and performed with correct alignment; safe modifications offered; no contraindicated poses for the audience. | Mostly correct alignment; safe choices. | One alignment or safety issue. | Multiple alignment/safety issues. | × 2 |
| **Breath co-ordination** | Clear breath cues throughout; breath leads movement; one explicit prāṇāyāma element. | Mostly co-ordinated; cues present. | Some breath cues; movement not aligned. | Breath ignored or chaotic. | × 1.5 |
| **Audience fit** | Routine clearly tailored to audience — pace, language, intent all match. | Audience considered; minor mismatch. | Routine works for one audience but pitched for another. | Audience not visibly considered. | × 1.5 |
| **Use of sources** | Cites at least one source aloud (verse, sūtra, or PDF reference). | Cites a source. | Mentions a source vaguely. | No citation. | × 1 |
| **Originality** | Pair's own thinking visible — a custom sequence, a unique cue, a memorable closing. | Clearly the pair's own work. | Largely standard sequence with minor variations. | Copied directly from class examples. | × 1 |
| **Communication / craft** | Clear, audible cues; smooth transitions; calm presence. | Communicates clearly. | Some unclear cues. | Hard to follow. | × 1 |

Maximum: 28. Project score = (sum × weights) × 30/28.

## Presentation marks (10)

| Criterion | 4 | 3 | 2 | 1 | Weight |
|-----------|---|---|---|---|--------|
| **Within time (5 min ± 30 sec)** | Within window | Within ± 1 min | Within ± 2 min | Outside | × 1 |
| **Both partners lead** | Equal share | Both speak unequally | One dominates | One silent | × 1 |
| **Class engagement (audience follows along)** | Clear, audible, paced for following | Most audience follows | Some confusion | Audience lost | × 0.5 |

Maximum: 10.

## Notes

- **Safety violations** (asking audience to do an inversion, advanced backbend, or breath retention) trigger immediate zero on **Āsana accuracy and safety**. The teacher quietly redirects mid-presentation.
- **Source citation** must be verbalised during the routine intro or close — not buried in handouts.
