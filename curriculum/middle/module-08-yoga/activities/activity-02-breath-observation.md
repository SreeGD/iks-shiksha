# Activity 02 — Breath Observation Practice

**Module:** Yoga · **Band:** Middle · **Day:** 6 · **Time:** 15 min within Day 6's 45

## Type

Seated breath practice + reflection worksheet

## Goal

Students complete 5 minutes of natural-breath observation followed by 5 minutes of counted breath (4 in, 4 out), and record their observations.

## Materials per student

- One mat
- Worksheet (below)

## Set-up (teacher, before class)

- Mats laid out. Quiet space.
- Soft bell for time markers.

## Instructions (student-facing handout)

> **Breath Observation**
>
> Two practices. Total 10 minutes of practice + 5 min for writing.
>
> **Practice 1: Natural Breath (5 min)**
>
> - Sit in sukhāsana. Eyes closed (or lowered).
> - Breathe completely naturally. Don't change anything.
> - Observe: which nostril feels more open? Is the breath deep or shallow? Even or uneven? Are there pauses?
> - Just NOTICE. Don't fix.
>
> **Practice 2: Counted Breath (5 min)**
>
> - Same seated position.
> - Inhale to a count of 4 (silent in your head).
> - Exhale to a count of 4.
> - Continuous — no holding at the top or bottom.
> - If you lose count, restart at 1.
>
> **After the practice — write:**

### Reflection worksheet

```

BEFORE the practice:
- My body felt: ___________________________________
- My mind felt: ___________________________________

DURING natural breath (Practice 1):
- One thing I noticed about my breath: ___________________________________
                                       ___________________________________

- Did your mind stay with the breath, or wander? __________________________

DURING counted breath (Practice 2):
- Easier or harder than natural breath? __________________________
- Did you lose count? If yes, how many times (roughly)? __________________________

AFTER the practice:
- My body felt: ___________________________________
- My mind felt: ___________________________________

One sentence: what surprised you?
______________________________________________________________________
______________________________________________________________________
```

## Discussion prompts (after writing — 5 min, whole class)

- Whose mind stayed with the breath the most? (No competition — celebrate honesty.)
- Did anyone find counted breath easier than natural? Why?
- Anyone notice a difference in their body before vs. after?

## Safety / sensitivity

- If a student becomes dizzy during counted breath, return to natural breath.
- If a student becomes anxious in silence, suggest "eyes open, gaze soft."
- **No forceful breathing.** No exception.

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Extend Practice 2 to 5-in, 5-out.
- **Tier down:** Skip Practice 2; do 8 min of natural-breath observation only.

## Clean-up

- Mats stacked. Worksheets collected for teacher's review.
