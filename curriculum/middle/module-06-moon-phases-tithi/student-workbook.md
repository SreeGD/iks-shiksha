## Day 2 — The Two Pakṣas

A lunar month has two halves:

| Sanskrit | English | Moon is |
|----------|---------|---------|
| *śukla pakṣa* | bright fortnight | waxing (growing) |
| *kṛṣṇa pakṣa* | dark fortnight | waning (shrinking) |

Two anchor days:

- *amāvasyā* — new moon. The Moon and Sun are in the same direction in the sky.
- *pūrṇimā* — full moon. The Moon is opposite the Sun, fully illuminated.
