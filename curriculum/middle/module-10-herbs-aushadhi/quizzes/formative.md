### Part A — answer key

| Q | Answer | Notes |
|---|--------|-------|

### Part B — answer key

| Q | Answer | Mark scheme |

| 1 | *adraka* (accept also *adrak*) | 1 |

## What to do with the results

- A class average below 6/10 on Part B = significant gaps. Reteach the chemistry headlines on Day 6 (push half of the ajwain content to Day 7).
- Individual students below 3/10 on Part B = pull aside on Day 7 for a 10-min one-on-one before the activity starts.
