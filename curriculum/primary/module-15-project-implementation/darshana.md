# Module 15 — Project Implementation — Capstone (Darśana)

**Sanskrit:** *Pañcagavya (and other capstones)* · **Band:** Primary (3-5) · **Duration:** 60 min · **Path:** Darśana (Workshop)

10-day applied project drawing on Modules 1-14.

> **Darśana note.** This workshop condenses Module 15's full 10-day arc into one hour. For the deeper version — daily lesson plans, quizzes, assessments, slide decks — switch to **[Adhyayana](../)** (the Full Course path).

---

## Opening hook · 5 min

Show the 14 module pictures. *"You've learned 14 things! Today: pick ONE and make a project!"*

---

## Core idea · 15 min

**You can make a project!**

Pick from this menu:
1. Make a 5-element collage (Module 2)
2. Build a doṣa-friend deck (Module 4)
3. Make a herb-friend deck (Module 10)
4. Draw a moon calendar (Module 6)
5. Build a sun calendar (Module 12)
6. Make a magic-square decoration (Module 14)
7. Design a kind-yoga sequence (Module 8)
8. Plant a tree (Module 13)

Or invent your own from a module you loved!

---

## Demonstration · 10 min

Show 3 sample projects (previous classes' work, if any, or teacher mock-ups). Children pick their favourite to inspire.

---

## Source moment · 5 min

Tell a story about a child who made a great project — and what made it great: choosing carefully, doing the work, showing pride.

---

## Hands-on activity · 15 min

Each child writes (or draws) their project plan: WHAT they'll make, what they need, who will help. Submit to teacher.

---

## Reflection + take-home · 10 min

Each child shares their project choice and why. Cheer for each commitment.
