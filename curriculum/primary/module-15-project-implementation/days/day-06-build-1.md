# Day 6 — Build / Make — Day 1

**Module:** Project Implementation — Capstone (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Start building the artefact (model, poster, deck, garden plot).

## Materials

- Notebook, pencils, drawing paper
- (See lesson-plan.md for any day-specific materials)

## The flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

Daily chant or breathing exercise.

### Story recall (4 min)

Briefly recall yesterday's lesson by asking: *"What's one thing you remember from yesterday?"*

### Today's idea (10 min)

- *siddhānta* (IAST: siddhānta; lit. "concluded principle or doctrine")

- TIME TO BUILD! 🛠️

Each child gets out their materials (paper, paint, plants, etc.) and starts.

Teacher walks around helping. Children share with their neighbour: *"What are you making?"*

End: hold up what you've started. *"Tomorrow we keep going!"*

### Activity (8 min)

A simplified version of *Mid-Project Critique Circle* (see `activities/`). Draw, sing, or act it out.

### Wrap-up (3 min)

Each child shares one word that describes today.
