# Lesson Plan — Project Implementation — Capstone (Primary)

10-day arc, 30 min per session.

### Day 1 — Capstone Overview and Project Menu

**Objective:** Review the 10-option project menu; pick a project.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (15 min) · Activity (8 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 2 — Scope and Plan

**Objective:** Write a 1-page project plan: problem, sources, deliverable, deadline.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (15 min) · Activity (8 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 3 — Research — Day 1

**Objective:** Gather sources; cross-reference earlier modules.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (15 min) · Activity (8 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 4 — Research — Day 2

**Objective:** Continue research; sketch initial design.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (15 min) · Activity (8 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 5 — Mid-Project Critique

**Objective:** Pairs swap plans; offer one strength + one concern.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (15 min) · Activity (8 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 6 — Build / Make — Day 1

**Objective:** Start building the artefact (model, poster, deck, garden plot).

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (15 min) · Activity (8 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 7 — Build / Make — Day 2

**Objective:** Continue building; daily journal entry.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (15 min) · Activity (8 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 8 — Document

**Objective:** Write up process, sources, reflections (~500 words for Senior).

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (15 min) · Activity (8 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 9 — Rehearse Presentation

**Objective:** Pair feedback on final presentation; refine.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (15 min) · Activity (8 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 10 — Project Share Day

**Objective:** Present project to peers + parents; complete self-assessment.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (15 min) · Activity (8 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

## Pacing notes

- Primary sessions are 30 min. Adjust if your block is different.
- Days 5 and 9 are buffer days — use to reinforce weaker concepts or extend stronger ones.
- The summative project (see `assessments/project-brief.md`) runs in parallel from Day 6 onward.

## Differentiation

- **For advanced students:** see `teacher-notes.md` for extension prompts.
- **For students needing more support:** simplify activities, do more pair-work, allow oral instead of written responses.
