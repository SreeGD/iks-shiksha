## Daily structure (30 min)

| Time | What |
|------|------|
| 3 min | Settle + chant |
| 10 min | Story / new content |
| 12 min | Drawing |
| 5 min | Sharing |

## Daily chant

> "Vāta, Pitta, Kapha — three friends inside me!"

## What children leave with

- 3 friends named
- A drawing of their friends with personalities
- 3 healthy-habit ideas: sleep on time, eat well, move every day
