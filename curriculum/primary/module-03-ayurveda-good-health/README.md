# Module 3 — Three Friends in Your Body (Ayurveda)

**Age band:** Primary · **Grades:** 3–5 · **Duration:** 10 sessions × 30 min

## What children will learn

Three friends live in everyone's body:

- **Vāta** — wind friend (quick, talkative, sometimes fidgety)
- **Pitta** — fire friend (sharp, focused, sometimes a bit fiery)
- **Kapha** — water/earth friend (calm, slow, steady)

Plus 3 healthy-habit ideas: **sleep on time, eat the right way, move every day**.

## How this module sounds

- Story-led. The three friends have personalities.
- Drawing every day. Children GIVE their friends names and faces.
- One Sanskrit word per friend, repeated daily.
