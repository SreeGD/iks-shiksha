# Module 3 — Āyurveda and Good Health (Darśana)

**Sanskrit:** *Āyurveda* · **Band:** Primary (3-5) · **Duration:** 60 min · **Path:** Darśana (Workshop)

The science of life — daily routine, diet, and well-being.

> **Darśana note.** This workshop condenses Module 3's full 10-day arc into one hour. For the deeper version — daily lesson plans, quizzes, assessments, slide decks — switch to **[Adhyayana](../)** (the Full Course path).

---

## Opening hook · 5 min

*"What time did you wake up today? Did you eat? What did you drink? Did you move?"* Quick show-of-hands. *"Āyurveda is just a name for: noticing these things and choosing well. Today we'll learn the BIG rules people in India have followed for thousands of years."*

---

## Core idea · 15 min

**Four big rules from Āyurveda:**

1. **Wake with the sun.** Sunrise = wake-up bell.
2. **Eat warm meals.** Cold food shocks the tummy.
3. **Sip water through the day.** Not ice-cold.
4. **Sleep when it's dark.** No screens before bed.

Each rule has a Sanskrit name (write on board):
- *dinacarya* (daily routine)
- *ahāra* (food)
- *nidrā* (sleep)
- *praheli* (water — drink slow)

Sing-chant: *"Wake. Eat. Sip. Sleep."*

---

## Demonstration · 10 min

**Six-taste tasting.** Set out (or describe) 6 small samples — honey (sweet), lemon (sour), salt water (salty), ginger (pungent), bitter gourd skin (bitter), banana peel water (astringent). Children sniff/taste tiny amounts. Match the Sanskrit name to each taste.

---

## Source moment · 5 min

Tell the story of Caraka, the wandering physician who walked from village to village teaching people: *"Wake up when birds wake up. Eat warm food. Be kind to your body."*

---

## Hands-on activity · 15 min

**Make a healthy day card.** Each child draws their ideal day: sunrise wake, warm breakfast, school, lunch with vegetables, play, dinner, bedtime story, sleep. Add Sanskrit labels for what they know.

---

## Reflection + take-home · 10 min

Each child says one Āyurveda rule they'll try this week. Cheer for each commitment.
