# Day 3 — Pitta, the Fire Friend

**Module:** Three Friends (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Children explore Pitta through warmth observation and stories.

## Materials

- A warm tube light or sunny window
- Drawing paper

## Flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

### Story (8 min)

*"Today: Pitta — the FIRE friend. Pitta is hot. Pitta helps cook your food inside you."*

*"What does Pitta do?"*
- Helps you digest lunch.
- Gives you energy.
- Makes your eyes see colours.

*"When Pitta is loud, you feel SHARP and FOCUSED. You can finish things. You're determined."*

*"When Pitta is TOO loud, you feel ANGRY or HUNGRY or HOT. Sometimes you snap at your friends."*

### Feel-the-fire activity (12 min)

- Stand at a sunny window or under a tube light.
- Hold your hand out. *"Feel the warmth? That's Pitta-like — outside warmth."*
- Touch your tummy. *"What's happening in here? Pitta is working on your lunch."*
- Touch your eyes (carefully). *"What do you see right now? Pitta makes seeing possible."*

*"To calm Pitta when it's too loud: cool water. Take a walk. Talk to a friend. Don't skip meals."*

### Drawing (5 min)

Draw your Pitta friend doing something focused.

### Show + chant (2 min)

## Homework

- Notice tomorrow: when does YOUR Pitta seem loud? (When hungry? When focused?)

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Try this: when you feel a bit angry tomorrow, drink water FIRST. Note what changes.
- **Tier down:** Remember: Pitta = fire = digestion + focus.
