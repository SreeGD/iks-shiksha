# Day 8 — Project Day 1: Make Your Friends

**Module:** Three Friends (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Each child makes their three friends with names, faces, and personalities.

## Materials

- Drawing paper / craft materials

## Flow

### Settle + chant + song (5 min)

### Project (22 min)

Each child:
- Draws their 3 friends (Vāta, Pitta, Kapha) on a SINGLE page.
- Gives each friend a NAME (any name — "Tornado," "Sparky," "Bumblebee," whatever).
- Gives each friend a FACE.
- Adds one thing each friend LIKES (e.g. Vāta = warm rice, Pitta = cucumber, Kapha = ginger tea).

### Show + chant (3 min)

## Homework

- Practise telling about your friends with a parent.
