# Day 6 — When Friends Rest (Sleep)

**Module:** Three Friends (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Children learn that the three friends need rest — and that sleep happens at certain times for a reason.

## Materials

- Drawing paper
- Optional: a "clock face" drawn on the board

## Flow

### Settle + chant + song (5 min)

### Story (10 min)

*"Just like you have a bedtime, the three friends have rest times."*

*"Pitta (fire friend) does her big work after you sleep — she fixes things inside your body while you rest. So if you sleep late, Pitta can't do her work properly."*

*"The friends like you to sleep BY 9 OR 10 at night, and wake UP with the sun."*

**Sleep rules from the three friends:**
- Sleep at the same time every night (friends like routine).
- Dim lights an hour before sleep (Pitta calms when it gets dark).
- No big meal right before sleep (friends are busy digesting; can't rest).
- Quiet wind-down (Vāta needs settling).

### Activity (10 min)

Each child draws their "best bedtime routine" in 4 panels:
1. After dinner
2. Brushing teeth
3. Quiet activity (reading, drawing)
4. Sleep

### Show + chant (5 min)

- 2 children share their drawings.
- Chant + song.

## Homework

- Try ONE bedtime rule tonight (dim lights early, OR no screens before bed, OR same time as last night). Note what happens.

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Try 2 bedtime rules.
- **Tier down:** Just remember: sleep at the same time, before 10pm.
