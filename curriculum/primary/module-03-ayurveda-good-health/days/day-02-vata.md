# Day 2 — Vāta, the Wind Friend

**Module:** Three Friends (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Children explore Vāta through movement and a game.

## Materials

- Open space
- Drawing paper

## Flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

### Story (5 min)

*"Today: meet Vāta — the WIND friend. Vāta makes things MOVE in your body."*

*"What does Vāta do?"*
- Your breath in and out — Vāta.
- Your heart beating — Vāta.
- Your hand waving — Vāta.
- Your thoughts moving — Vāta.

### Vāta game (15 min)

**Movement game.** Three rounds:
1. *Wind round:* run around the room like the wind for 30 seconds.
2. *Quick-turn round:* stop and change direction every 5 seconds.
3. *Breath round:* sit. Breathe FAST 10 times. (How does your body feel? Vāta is awake.)

Then settle. *"That was your Vāta friend being LOUD. When Vāta is loud, we feel quick, talkative, sometimes worried."*

*"To calm Vāta: SLOW breath. Warm food. Sit still. Hug something soft."*

### Drawing (5 min)

Draw your Vāta friend doing something quick.

### Show + chant (2 min)

## Homework

- Notice tonight: when does YOUR Vāta seem loud? (Before sleep? When excited?)

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Try the "calm Vāta" practices tonight before bed.
- **Tier down:** Just remember: Vāta = wind = movement.
