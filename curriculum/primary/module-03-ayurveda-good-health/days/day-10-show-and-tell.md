# Day 10 — Show & Tell

**Module:** Three Friends (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Each child shows their three friends. Sticker for all.

## Flow

### Settle + chant + song (5 min)

Biggest chant + song yet!

### Show & Tell (22 min)

- 1 child at a time. 30 seconds. Chime.
- Each child:
  1. Names their three friends.
  2. Tells one thing each friend likes.
  3. Says which friend is usually loudest in them.

Sticker for all.

### Closing (3 min)

- Sing the song one last time.
- *"Your three friends are with you for life. Take care of them."*

## Homework

- None. Module done.
