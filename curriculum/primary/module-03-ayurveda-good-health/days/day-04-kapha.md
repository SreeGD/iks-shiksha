# Day 4 — Kapha, the Water Friend

**Module:** Three Friends (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Children explore Kapha through stillness and a calming practice.

## Materials

- A glass of water
- Drawing paper

## Flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

### Story (8 min)

*"Today: Kapha — the WATER-and-EARTH friend. Kapha makes you strong and steady."*

*"What does Kapha do?"*
- Holds your bones together.
- Makes your skin smooth.
- Helps you feel calm.

*"When Kapha is loud, you feel CALM, patient, steady. You're a good friend who listens. You sleep well."*

*"When Kapha is TOO loud, you feel sleepy, slow, or stuck. Sometimes you don't want to get up."*

### Be the water (12 min)

Show the glass of water. *"See how it's still? That's Kapha-like."*

**Stillness practice:**
- Sit cross-legged. Hands on knees.
- Eyes closed (or lowered).
- Be still for 1 minute. Count 10 breaths.

How did it feel? Most children find it harder than they expected.

*"Kapha is steady but stillness isn't always easy. You have to practise."*

*"To wake up Kapha when it's too sleepy: exercise. Bright light. Hot drink. Spicy food. A surprise dance party."*

### Drawing (5 min)

Draw your Kapha friend doing something calm.

### Show + chant (2 min)

## Homework

- Notice: when do YOU feel most Kapha-like? (Morning? After a big meal? When happy?)

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Sit still for 2 minutes at home tonight.
- **Tier down:** Just remember: Kapha = water = calm + steady.
