# Day 5 — Friend Song + Daily Check-In

**Module:** Three Friends (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Children memorise the 3 friends via a song + learn the daily "which friend is loudest?" check-in.

## Materials

- 3 picture cards

## Flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

### Song (10 min)

Tune: "Twinkle Twinkle":

> Vāta, Pitta, Kapha three,
> Three good friends inside of me!
> Vāta is the wind that flies,
> Pitta is the fire in my eyes,
> Kapha is the steady ground —
> All three friends in me are found!

Sing 3 times. Add gestures (Vāta = wave arms; Pitta = touch tummy; Kapha = pat shoulders).

### Daily check-in game (12 min)

Show each card. Ask:
- *"WHICH friend feels loudest in you RIGHT NOW?"*

Each child whispers their answer to a partner.

Then:
- *"WHICH friend felt loudest YESTERDAY at this time?"*
- *"WHICH friend felt loudest THIS MORNING when you woke up?"*

Note out loud: *"The same friend isn't always loudest. They change with the day. That's normal."*

### Drawing (3 min)

Quick: write all 3 friend names on one page.

### Show + chant + song (2 min)

## Homework

- Sing the song with a parent tonight.

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Make up verse 2.
- **Tier down:** Just sing along.
