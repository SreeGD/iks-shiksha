# Day 1 — Three Friends Story

**Module:** Three Friends (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Children meet all three friends (Vāta, Pitta, Kapha) through a story.

## Materials

- 3 picture cards (wind blowing leaves; sun/candle; water/clouds)
- Drawing paper

## Flow

### Settle + chant intro (5 min)

Teach: *"Vāta, Pitta, Kapha — three friends inside me!"*

3 times, with claps.

### Story (10 min)

*"Inside every body — yours, mine, your grandma's — live THREE FRIENDS. They've been there since you were born. Today we meet them."*

Show each card with a story:

**Vāta — Wind Friend.** *"Vāta is the wind friend. She makes things move inside you — your breath, your heartbeat, your thoughts. People with lots of Vāta are quick and talkative. Sometimes they fidget a lot."*

**Pitta — Fire Friend.** *"Pitta is the fire friend. He helps your food become energy. People with lots of Pitta are sharp and focused. Sometimes they get a bit fiery (angry)."*

**Kapha — Water Friend.** *"Kapha is the water-and-earth friend. She makes you strong and steady. People with lots of Kapha are calm and patient. Sometimes they're a bit slow."*

*"Important: we ALL have ALL THREE friends. Nobody has just one. Sometimes one friend is louder than the others — that's normal!"*

### Drawing (12 min)

Each child draws their THREE friends — with faces, names, anything they like!

### Show + chant (3 min)

2 children share. Chant to close.

## Homework

- Tell a family member about the three friends. Ask them which friend they think is loudest in YOU today.

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Draw all 3 friends doing something together.
- **Tier down:** Draw one favourite friend.
