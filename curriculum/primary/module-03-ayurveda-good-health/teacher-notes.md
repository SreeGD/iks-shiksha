# Teacher Notes — Module 3 (Primary)

## Tone

Three friends with personalities. Not types. Not labels. Not medicine.

## Hard rule

**Never imply your child IS a Vāta type / has a Pitta imbalance / etc.** The three friends are everyone's friends.

If a child says "my mum says I have Pitta," gently redirect: "Yes! And you have Vāta and Kapha too. They're all your friends."

## Common things to handle

- **"My friend has bad behaviour because their Pitta is angry."** Reframe: friends are NOT labels. We don't blame our friends or each other.
- **"My grandma uses Ayurveda for my cough."** Celebrate. *"Your family knows the friends well."*

## Safety

No herbs without parent confirmation of no allergies. No food samples. No medical advice in any form.

## If you only have 6 days

- Day 1: Story + Vāta
- Day 2: Pitta + Kapha
- Day 3: Song
- Day 4: Sleep + food (combined)
- Day 5: Project
- Day 6: Show & Tell
