# Three Friends in Your Body

Ayurveda for Little Ones · Grades 3–5

---

# The chant

> "Vāta, Pitta, Kapha — three friends inside me!"

Say it every day.

---

# Vāta — the WIND friend

Quick. Talkative. Sometimes fidgety.

Does: breath, heartbeat, movement, thoughts.

---

# Pitta — the FIRE friend

Sharp. Focused. Sometimes fiery.

Does: digestion, energy, seeing, courage.

---

# Kapha — the WATER friend

Calm. Steady. Sometimes slow.

Does: strong bones, smooth skin, peaceful feelings.

---

# Everyone has all three

You. Me. Your grandma. Your dog.

ALL THREE friends, ALL the time.

Sometimes one is louder than the others.

---

# Sleep makes friends rest

Friends like to rest BY 9 or 10 at night.

They wake up with the sun.

Same bedtime every day = happy friends.

---

# Food for friends

Variety on your plate = happy friends.

Six tastes:
sweet, sour, salty, spicy, bitter, astringent.

A traditional Indian thali has all six!

---

# Your project

Draw your THREE friends.

Names, faces, what they like.

Day 10 = Show & Tell. Sticker for all!

---

# Thank you

Sing the song one more time!
