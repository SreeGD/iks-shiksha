# Activity 01 — Which Friend Is Loudest?

**Module:** Three Friends (Primary) · **Reusable** · **Time:** 5 min

## Goal

Children practise the daily "check-in" with their three friends.

## Instructions

Stand in a circle.

Teacher calls one of these scenarios. Children point to a card (or say): which friend is loudest?

1. *"You're running to school and are late."* (Vāta!)
2. *"You're hungry and lunch isn't ready."* (Pitta!)
3. *"You're snuggled under a blanket on a rainy day."* (Kapha!)
4. *"You're focused on a puzzle."* (Pitta!)
5. *"You can't sit still."* (Vāta!)
6. *"You're falling asleep listening to a story."* (Kapha!)
7. *"You're talking really fast."* (Vāta!)
8. *"You feel calm and patient."* (Kapha!)

## Discussion

- Did you ever feel TWO friends loud at the same time?
- Has the loudest friend changed today?
