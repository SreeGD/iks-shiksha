# Parent Guide — Module 3 (Primary): Three Friends

**Your child's age:** Primary (Grades 3–5) · **Module length:** 10 sessions × 30 min

## What your child is learning

Three friends from Ayurveda — Vāta, Pitta, Kapha — presented as personality friends in everyone's body. By Day 10 your child can name all three and tell a 30-second story about one.

Plus 3 healthy-habit ideas: **sleep on time, eat well, move every day**.

## Safety note — important

**Nothing in this module is medical advice.** The three friends are a fun introduction to a 2000-year-old framework. We are NOT telling your child what their "type" is. We are NOT recommending treatments. If your child is unwell, see a doctor.

## What to expect

- Story + drawing every day.
- A daily chant: *"Vāta, Pitta, Kapha — three friends inside me!"*
- A Day 10 show-and-tell project (drawings or models of their friends).

## How to help

**Helpful:**
- Ask at dinner: "Which friend was loudest today?" (Fidgety = Vāta; intense = Pitta; calm/sleepy = Kapha.)
- Share family wellness traditions.
- Help with project: paper, crayons, recycled junk for models.

**Not helpful:**
- Saying "you're a Vāta type." We don't label people.
- Using the module as a reason to push lifestyle changes on your child.
- Confusing this introduction with actual Ayurvedic medicine (which is a long, qualified field of practice).

## What success looks like

- Your child names all 3 friends.
- They tell you ONE thing each friend likes.
- They give you a sense of their drawings.
