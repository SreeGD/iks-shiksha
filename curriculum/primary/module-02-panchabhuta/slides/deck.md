# The Five Friends

A story about Pṛthvī, Ap, Tejas, Vāyu, and Ākāśa

Primary · Grades 3–5

---

# Meet the friends

🌍 **Pṛthvī** — earth
💧 **Ap** — water
🔥 **Tejas** — fire / warmth
💨 **Vāyu** — air / wind
🌌 **Ākāśa** — space

---

# Our chant

> *Pṛthvī, ap, tejas, vāyu, ākāśa*
> Earth, water, fire, air, space!

Sing it 3 times every day.

---

# Pṛthvī — Earth

Solid things. Rocks. Floor. Your bones.

Anything you can stand on or hold.

---

# Ap — Water

Liquid things. Rivers. Tea. Tears.

Anything that flows.

---

# Tejas — Fire / Warmth

Heat. Light. Sun. Candle.

Even the warmth of your own hand.

---

# Vāyu — Air

Wind. Breath. The air in a balloon.

You can't see it — you can feel it.

---

# Ākāśa — Space

The empty room.

The gap between your hands.

The space where everything else lives.

---

# Five qualities

| Element | Number of qualities |
|---------|---------------------|
| Pṛthvī | 5 |
| Ap | 4 |
| Tejas | 3 |
| Vāyu | 2 |
| Ākāśa | 1 |

Earth has all the qualities. Space has just one — sound.

(For older children — Grade 5)

---

# Sort it!

In pairs, sort 5 objects into the 5 element trays.

If something fits TWO elements — pick the strongest, then name the others.

---

# Sometimes elements are out of balance

- Too much air? = storm
- Too little water? = drought
- Too much earth (wrong kind)? = plastic waste
- Too noisy space? = traffic noise

We notice. We help where we can.

---

# Your project

Pick ONE element.

Make: a drawing, a model, OR a 30-second story.

Show how YOU see this element at home.

---

# Day 10 — Show and tell

30 seconds each.

Name your element.
Show your project.
Say one place at home you see it.

Then — a sticker for everyone!
