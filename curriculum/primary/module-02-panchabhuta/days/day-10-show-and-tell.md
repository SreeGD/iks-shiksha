# Day 10 — Show + Tell

**Module:** Panchabhūta (Primary) · **Time:** 35 min

## Goal

Each child stands up, shows their project, and tells about it for 30 seconds. They get a sticker.

## Materials

- All children's projects
- Stickers (one per child)
- Bell or chime to gently signal 30 seconds

## The flow

### Settle + chant + song (5 min)

The biggest, loudest singing of the chant. *"This is the last day we'll meet the five friends together. Sing big!"*

### Show + tell (25 min — most of the class)

- 1 child at a time. 30 seconds. Chime when time's up.
- Each child says (with teacher's prompting if needed):
  1. The element name.
  2. What their project shows.
  3. One example from home.

- Class claps. Child gets a sticker.

With ~25 children in 25 minutes, plan tight pacing. If you have more children, split across two days OR pair them up.

### Closing ceremony (5 min)

- Whole class stands.
- Final chant — biggest yet.
- Teacher: *"You all met the five friends. They live with you now."*

## Homework

- None. Module done.

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** A child who's confident can give a 45-sec show-and-tell with an extra detail.
- **Tier down:** Shy child sits next to the teacher; teacher describes the project for them.

## Teacher's quick notes

- The 30-second cap is for everyone, including the chatty ones. The chime helps.
- This is a celebration day. No "constructive feedback" — only stickers and applause.
- Take a class photo of all the projects laid out. Send to parents.
