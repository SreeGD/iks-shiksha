# Day 9 — Project Day 2

**Module:** Panchabhūta (Primary) · **Time:** 35 min

## Goal

Each child finishes their project and practises their 30-second show-and-tell.

## Materials

- All in-progress materials
- Story-tellers' notes (optional)

## The flow

### Settle + chant + song (5 min)

### Finish + practise (25 min)

- **First 15 min: finish.** Children complete drawings, models, or stories.
- **Last 10 min: practise.** Children rehearse their 30-sec show-and-tell with a partner. The partner listens for: did they say the element name? Did they show one example from home?

### Show + chant (5 min)

2 volunteers share their final piece. Class chants the element name in response.

## Homework

- Bring your project tomorrow! Practise telling about it one more time.

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Practise your show-and-tell with three different listeners — get three pieces of feedback.
- **Tier down:** Just one practice with the teacher.

## Teacher's quick notes

- Some children will have lost or forgotten their work-in-progress. Have spare materials ready to remake quickly.
- The 30-second show-and-tell is the FOCUSED skill of Day 10. Practise the timing today.
