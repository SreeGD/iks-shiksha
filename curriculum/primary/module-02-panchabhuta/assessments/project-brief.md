# Project — Module 2 Panchabhūta (Primary)

## What you'll make

Pick ONE of the five friends (Pṛthvī, Ap, Tejas, Vāyu, Ākāśa). Make something that shows that friend AND one place at your home where you see it.

## Three options

1. **A drawing** — big picture (whole page).
2. **A model** — clay, recycled junk (boxes, bottles, fabric).
3. **A story** — 30 seconds, told aloud.

## Schedule

- **Day 7:** decide your element.
- **Day 8:** start.
- **Day 9:** finish + practise.
- **Day 10:** show + tell. 30 seconds each. You get a sticker.

## What we'll look for (simplified rubric)

| | What we celebrate |
|---|---|
| ⭐ | You named your element |
| ⭐⭐ | You showed your element with a clear picture / model / story |
| ⭐⭐⭐ | You named one place at home where you see your element |

All three stars = "complete project!" Two stars = "lovely effort!" One star = "great start!"

No failures here. Every child gets a sticker.
