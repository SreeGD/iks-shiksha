# Day 5 — Twelve Rāśis

**Module:** Movement of the Sun (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Name the 12 rāśis in Sanskrit and pair with Greek zodiac names.

## Materials

- Notebook, pencils, drawing paper
- (See lesson-plan.md for any day-specific materials)

## The flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

Daily chant or breathing exercise.

### Story recall (4 min)

Briefly recall yesterday's lesson by asking: *"What's one thing you remember from yesterday?"*

### Today's idea (10 min)

- *rāśi* (IAST: rāśi; lit. "1/12th of the ecliptic; zodiacal sign")

- The Sun visits 12 special "houses" each year!

Names (sing-chant): *"Meṣa, Vṛṣabha, Mithuna, Karka, Siṁha, Kanyā, Tulā, Vṛścika, Dhanus, Makara, Kumbha, Mīna!"*

Each "house" has a Sanskrit name AND a Greek name:
- Meṣa = Aries (ram!), Vṛṣabha = Taurus (bull!), Mithuna = Gemini (twins!)...

Children pick one rāśi to draw. Today's question: *"What's YOUR birth-month rāśi?"*

### Activity (8 min)

A simplified version of *Sunrise Direction Tracking* (see `activities/`). Draw, sing, or act it out.

### Wrap-up (3 min)

Each child shares one word that describes today.
