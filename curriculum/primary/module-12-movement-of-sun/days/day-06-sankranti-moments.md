# Day 6 — Saṁkrānti Moments

**Module:** Movement of the Sun (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Identify Makara, Karka, and other saṁkrāntis on the calendar.

## Materials

- Notebook, pencils, drawing paper
- (See lesson-plan.md for any day-specific materials)

## The flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

Daily chant or breathing exercise.

### Story recall (4 min)

Briefly recall yesterday's lesson by asking: *"What's one thing you remember from yesterday?"*

### Today's idea (10 min)

- *sāyana* (IAST: sāyana; lit. "tropical zodiac (linked to seasons)")

- When the Sun moves from one rāśi-house to the next, we call it *Saṅkrānti*.
- The most famous: *Makara Saṅkrānti* — when the Sun enters Makara (Capricorn). Around January 14 in many years!

Show a wheel with 12 houses. Children put a Sun-sticker on each rāśi, then circle the date when the Sun enters Makara.

*"In Tamil Nadu, this is Pongal. In Punjab, Lohri. In many places, it's a happy festival!"*

### Activity (8 min)

A simplified version of *Sunrise Direction Tracking* (see `activities/`). Draw, sing, or act it out.

### Wrap-up (3 min)

Each child shares one word that describes today.
