# Day 9 — Balance and Imbalance

**Module:** Doshas — Vāta, Pitta, Kapha (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Distinguish prakṛti (innate) from vikṛti (current state); recognise simple imbalance signs.

## Materials

- Notebook, pencils, drawing paper
- (See lesson-plan.md for any day-specific materials)

## The flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

Daily chant or breathing exercise.

### Story recall (4 min)

Briefly recall yesterday's lesson by asking: *"What's one thing you remember from yesterday?"*

### Today's idea (10 min)

- *pitta* (IAST: pitta; lit. "transformation principle (fire, tejas+ap)")

- Sometimes one friend gets too loud!
  - Wiggle too loud = jittery, can't sit still, dry mouth
  - Spark too loud = grumpy, hot, hangry
  - Cozy too loud = sleepy, heavy, sad

Story: *"Imagine if all three friends are at a party. If one shouts too much, the others get tired."* Ask: *"What helps Wiggle calm down? What helps Spark cool off? What helps Cozy wake up?"*

Each child draws one friend in calm mode and one in too-loud mode.

### Activity (8 min)

A simplified version of *Build a Doṣa Rhythm Clock* (see `activities/`). Draw, sing, or act it out.

### Wrap-up (3 min)

Each child shares one word that describes today.
