# Day 5 — Mapping to the Five Elements

**Module:** Doshas — Vāta, Pitta, Kapha (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Connect each doṣa to its two constituent elements (vāta=ākāśa+vāyu, pitta=tejas+ap, kapha=ap+pṛthvī).

## Materials

- Notebook, pencils, drawing paper
- (See lesson-plan.md for any day-specific materials)

## The flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

Daily chant or breathing exercise.

### Story recall (4 min)

Briefly recall yesterday's lesson by asking: *"What's one thing you remember from yesterday?"*

### Today's idea (10 min)

- *prakṛti* (IAST: prakṛti; lit. "innate constitution")

Introduce the concept gently using a story, a drawing, or a sensory example. The concept for today: **Mapping to the Five Elements**. Keep sentences short.

### Activity (8 min)

A simplified version of *Build a Doṣa Rhythm Clock* (see `activities/`). Draw, sing, or act it out.

### Wrap-up (3 min)

Each child shares one word that describes today.
