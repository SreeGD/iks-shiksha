# Day 6 — Prakṛti Self-Assessment

**Module:** Doshas — Vāta, Pitta, Kapha (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Use a worksheet to estimate own dominant doṣa(s) — emphasising NOT diagnosis.

## Materials

- Notebook, pencils, drawing paper
- (See lesson-plan.md for any day-specific materials)

## The flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

Daily chant or breathing exercise.

### Story recall (4 min)

Briefly recall yesterday's lesson by asking: *"What's one thing you remember from yesterday?"*

### Today's idea (10 min)

- *vikṛti* (IAST: vikṛti; lit. "current imbalance")

- Today: which friend is most like ME right now?

Ask three questions:
1. *"When you talk to friends, do you feel quick (Wiggle), focused (Spark), or steady (Cozy)?"*
2. *"At meal time, are you usually nibbling little bits (W), eating fast and hot (S), or eating slow and big (C)?"*
3. *"At sleep time, do you flip around (W), sleep just enough (S), or sleep deep (C)?"*

Each child colours a small chart with one star for each friend that fits them today. *"All three friends live in everyone. Today's stars are just for today — they can change tomorrow."*

### Activity (8 min)

A simplified version of *Build a Doṣa Rhythm Clock* (see `activities/`). Draw, sing, or act it out.

### Wrap-up (3 min)

Each child shares one word that describes today.
