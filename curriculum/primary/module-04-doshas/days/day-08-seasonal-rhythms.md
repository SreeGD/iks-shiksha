# Day 8 — Doṣa Rhythms Across Seasons

**Module:** Doshas — Vāta, Pitta, Kapha (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Map doṣa cycles to monsoon/summer/winter.

## Materials

- Notebook, pencils, drawing paper
- (See lesson-plan.md for any day-specific materials)

## The flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

Daily chant or breathing exercise.

### Story recall (4 min)

Briefly recall yesterday's lesson by asking: *"What's one thing you remember from yesterday?"*

### Today's idea (10 min)

- *vāta* (IAST: vāta; lit. "motion principle (wind, ākāśa+vāyu)")

- Each season has a special friend:
  - **Spring** — Cozy is busy melting away! (Allergies, sniffles.)
  - **Summer** — Spark is hot! (Sweating, drinking water.)
  - **Winter** — Wiggle is dry and cool! (Dry skin, blankets.)

Draw a tree in three seasons (bare → blossom → leaves) and write which friend lives there. Ask: *"What did our family eat in summer? In winter? Why?"*

### Activity (8 min)

A simplified version of *Build a Doṣa Rhythm Clock* (see `activities/`). Draw, sing, or act it out.

### Wrap-up (3 min)

Each child shares one word that describes today.
