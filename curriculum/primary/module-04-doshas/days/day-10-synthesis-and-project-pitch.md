# Day 10 — Synthesis and Project Pitch

**Module:** Doshas — Vāta, Pitta, Kapha (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Pitch the 7-day self-observation project and answer assessment quiz.

## Materials

- Notebook, pencils, drawing paper
- (See lesson-plan.md for any day-specific materials)

## The flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

Daily chant or breathing exercise.

### Story recall (4 min)

Briefly recall yesterday's lesson by asking: *"What's one thing you remember from yesterday?"*

### Today's idea (10 min)

- *kapha* (IAST: kapha; lit. "structure principle (cohesion, ap+pṛthvī)")

- Today we say goodbye to the three friends — but they will stay with us!

Each child shares one favourite friend and one thing they learned about that friend. Make a class wall: three big drawings (Wiggle, Spark, Cozy) with each child's small drawing of them stuck around the big one.

Sing the friends-song together: *"Wiggle, Spark, Cozy — three friends inside, three friends inside, three friends inside me!"*

### Activity (8 min)

A simplified version of *Build a Doṣa Rhythm Clock* (see `activities/`). Draw, sing, or act it out.

### Wrap-up (3 min)

Each child shares one word that describes today.
