# Project — Module 5 (Primary)

## What you'll make

In pairs, design and build a "Make 10" game. Anything goes: cards, board game, dice game, jumping game. The only rule: somewhere in your game, players have to find or use pairs that add to 10.

## Schedule

- **Day 7:** sketch your game.
- **Day 8:** build it.
- **Day 9:** test with a buddy pair.
- **Day 10:** play each other's games at the Showcase.

## What we'll celebrate (simplified rubric)

| | What we celebrate |
|---|---|
| ⭐ | Your game has the "Make 10" idea in it |
| ⭐⭐ | The rules are clear enough that another pair can read and play |
| ⭐⭐⭐ | Your game is fun to play — at least one buddy-pair-member smiled |

All three stars = sticker + photo for parents.
