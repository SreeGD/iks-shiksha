## Why we don't teach the full dot method at Primary

The dot method requires:
- Fluent 10-pair recall (foundational)
- Scanning a column for groups (intermediate)
- Place-value understanding for the carry (intermediate)

Most Grade 3–4 children are still building the first. We focus there and tease the rest.

## For families who ask

The Bindu Paddhati ("dot method") is a mental-arithmetic technique taught in Indian schools. We introduce its building blocks now; the full method comes in Grade 6–8.
