# Day 8 — Project Day 1

**Module:** Make Ten (Primary) · **Time:** 35 min

## Goal

Each pair builds a playable version of their game.

## Materials

- Cards, dice, drawing paper, markers, glue, scissors

## The flow

### Settle + chant (5 min)

### Project work (25 min)

- Pairs work to build their game:
  - Make the cards / board
  - Write the rules clearly (so another pair can read and play)
  - Decorate if there's time

Teacher rotates: 60 seconds per pair, ONE question: *"Can another pair play your game with just the rules?"*

### Share progress (3 min)

2 pairs hold up their game.

### Show + chant (2 min)

Daily chant.

## Homework

- Finish at home what you didn't finish in class. Tomorrow we play!

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Add a "second round" or "challenge" level to your game.
- **Tier down:** Keep it simple — fewer cards, fewer rules.

## Teacher's quick notes

- The "rules clear enough for another pair" criterion is the actual project standard.
- Spend extra time on pairs whose game has unclear rules — most common issue.
