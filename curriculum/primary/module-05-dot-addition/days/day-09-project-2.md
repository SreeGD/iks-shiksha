# Day 9 — Project Day 2

**Module:** Make Ten (Primary) · **Time:** 35 min

## Goal

Each pair finishes their game and tests it with another pair.

## Materials

- All in-progress games

## The flow

### Settle + chant (5 min)

### Final touches (15 min)

Pairs complete their game. Rules card, board, all pieces ready.

### Test swap (12 min)

- Pair up with a buddy pair. Swap games.
- Read each other's rules. Play their game for 5 minutes.
- Then give feedback: 1 thing that worked, 1 thing that was confusing.

### Show + chant (3 min)

- 2 pairs share what they learned from testing.
- Daily chant.

## Homework

- Make any final fixes. Bring everything tomorrow!

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Add a sticker / reward system to your game design.
- **Tier down:** Simpler rules. Less is more.

## Teacher's quick notes

- The test swap is the real test of clarity. Pairs whose game is hard to follow now have evidence.
- Help pairs who realise their rules are too complex — encourage them to *simplify* not redesign.
