# Make Ten!

Primary · Grades 3–5

---

# The five 10-pairs

| 1+9 | 2+8 | 3+7 | 4+6 | 5+5 |
|---|---|---|---|---|

Sing it:

> "1 and 9 make 10! 2 and 8 make 10! 3 and 7 make 10! 4 and 6 make 10! 5 and 5 make 10!"

---

# Why?

If you can find 10-pairs in a list, you can add MUCH faster.

Adding to 10 is easy.

Adding random numbers is hard.

---

# Find the pair (try it)

```
3  7  2  5  8  1  4  6
```

How many 10-pairs?

(Answer: 3 + 7, 2 + 8, 4 + 6 — three pairs! Leftover: 5, 1)

---

# Add by grouping

```
6
3
7
5
4
2
```

Find pairs first: 6+4=10, 3+7=10. Leftover: 5+2=7.

Total: 10+10+7 = **27**.

Faster than adding one-by-one!

# The Add-9 Trick

```
9 = 10 - 1
33 + 9 = 33 + 10 - 1 = 42
```

Add 10. Take 1 away. Done.

Try: 47 + 9 = ?

# Meet the Dot

The Sanskrit name: ***Bindu Paddhati***

It's a trick for adding LONG columns of LONG numbers.

You'll learn it fully in Grade 6.

Today — just watch a demo!
