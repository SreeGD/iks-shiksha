# Activity 01 — The Animal Yoga Walk

**Module:** Yoga (Primary) · **Day:** 6 · **Time:** 18 min within Day 6's 30

## Goal

Children flow through the 5 poses + bhrāmarī in sequence.

## Materials

- Mats / floor space

## Sequence (one round)

| Time | Pose | Animal cue | Note |
|------|------|------------|------|
| 20 sec | Mountain (tāḍāsana) | - | Stand still and tall |
| 15 sec | Tree right (vṛkṣāsana) | Flamingo | Foot on calf, not knee |
| 15 sec | Tree left (vṛkṣāsana) | Flamingo | Switch sides |
| 60 sec | Child's pose (balāsana) | Sleeping puppy | Forehead to mat |
| 30 sec (5 waves) | Cat-Cow | Cat + cow | Breathe with each wave |
| 30 sec (3 rounds) | Bumblebee | Bee | Hum on the exhale |
| 20 sec | Mountain | - | Close |

Total: about 3.5 min per round.

## How to use

- Round 1: very slow, teacher calls each pose.
- Round 2: slightly faster, children lead themselves.
- Round 3 (optional): a brave child volunteer calls out.

## Safety

- Vṛkṣāsana foot ABOVE or BELOW the standing knee, NEVER on it.
- Cat-cow: gentle. No deep backbend.
- Bhrāmarī: skip if asthma; replace with 3 natural breaths.

## Clean-up

- Mats stacked.
