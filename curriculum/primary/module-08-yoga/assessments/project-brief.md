# Project — Module 8 Yoga (Primary)

## What you'll make

Invent ONE yoga pose. Give it a name. Teach it to the class for 1 minute on Day 10.

## Rules

- The pose must be SAFE — no jumping, no flips, no putting weight on your head.
- You must be able to HOLD the pose for 10 seconds.
- The name can be anything — Sanskrit-sounding, an animal, a feeling, a made-up word.

## Schedule

- **Day 7:** invent + try.
- **Day 8:** draw + name.
- **Day 9:** practise teaching with a partner.
- **Day 10:** teach the class for 1 minute. Sticker!

## What we'll celebrate

| | What we celebrate |
|---|---|
| ⭐ | Your pose has a name |
| ⭐⭐ | You showed it clearly to the class |
| ⭐⭐⭐ | The class could try it along with you |

All three stars = sticker + photo for parents.
