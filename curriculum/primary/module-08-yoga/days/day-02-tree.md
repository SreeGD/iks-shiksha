# Day 2 — The Tree (vṛkṣāsana)

**Module:** Yoga (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Children try the tree pose. Wobble is welcome.

## Materials

- Mats
- Picture of a tree (and a flamingo)

## The flow

### Settle + opening tāḍāsana (3 min)

5 breaths, then 30 sec mountain pose.

### Story (5 min)

*"Today's pose is **vṛkṣāsana** — tree pose. A tree stands tall on one root. Today YOU'll stand tall on one foot."*

*"Trees sway a little. So will you. That's totally OK."*

### Tree practice (15 min)

- *"Start in mountain. Move your weight onto your LEFT foot."*
- *"Now bend your RIGHT knee. Put the SOLE of your right foot against the INSIDE of your LEFT calf. Or below the knee. NOT on the knee."*
- *"Hands at heart — palms together. Or raise them like branches over your head."*
- *"Look at one spot on the wall and keep looking at it."*
- *"Breathe like a calm tree."*
- Hold 15 sec. Switch sides. Hold 15 sec.
- Repeat 3 times each side.

If children wobble: *"Trees sway in the wind! Wobble is what balance feels like when it's learning."*

If children fall: laugh together. *"Try again."*

### Reflection (4 min)

- *"Which side was easier?"*
- *"What did your eyes look at?"*

### Close (3 min)

Tāḍāsana. Namaste.

## Homework

- Stand on one leg at home for 10 seconds. Switch. Repeat. (Brush teeth while doing it!)

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Hold for 30 sec. Try with eyes closed (briefly).
- **Tier down:** Use a wall behind you for support. Or rest the lifted foot on the floor next to the standing foot.

## Teacher's quick notes

- THE knee safety cue is critical. Foot above OR below the knee. NEVER ON the side of the knee.
- Wobble is information. Falling is information. Both are fine. The skill is *trying again*.
