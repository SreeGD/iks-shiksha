# Day 6 — Animal Yoga Walk

**Module:** Yoga (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Children flow through all 5 poses + bhrāmarī as a sequence, animal-style.

## Materials

- Mats / open floor

## The flow

### Settle + opening tāḍāsana (3 min)

### Demo the sequence (5 min)

- *"Today we put EVERYTHING together. The Animal Yoga Walk."*
- *"Mountain → Flamingo (Tree, both sides) → Sleeping Puppy (Child's pose) → Cat-Cow waves → Bumblebee → back to Mountain."*

### Practice the flow (18 min)

Round 1: very slow, teacher calling each pose.

```
Mountain — 20 sec
Flamingo (tree pose, right) — 15 sec
Flamingo (tree pose, left) — 15 sec
Sleeping Puppy (child's pose) — 60 sec
Cat-Cow — 5 waves
Bumblebee Breath — 3 rounds
Mountain — 20 sec
```

Total: about 4 min per round.

Round 2: slightly faster, children leading themselves.

Optional Round 3: a brave child volunteers to call out the sequence.

### Reflection (3 min)

- *"Which animal felt best in your body?"*
- *"Which one was hardest?"*

### Close (1 min)

Tāḍāsana. Namaste.

## Homework

- Practice the full sequence at home — your favourite part TWICE.

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Add 5 more cat-cow rounds OR 5 more bumblebee breaths.
- **Tier down:** Skip the bumblebee if shy or unwell. Replace with 3 natural breaths in seated.

## Teacher's quick notes

- This is the practice rehearsal for the project. Children should know the FLOW now.
- Day 7 they'll INVENT a pose to add — preview it: "tomorrow, you'll make UP a pose and give it a name!"
