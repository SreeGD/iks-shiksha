# Day 9 — Project Day 2 (Practise Teaching)

**Module:** Yoga (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Each child rehearses their 1-minute pose teaching.

## Materials

- Mats
- Pose drawings

## The flow

### Settle + opening tāḍāsana (3 min)

### Rehearsal (20 min)

- Children pair up.
- Each child teaches their pose to their partner for 1 minute:
  - Name the pose.
  - Show the pose.
  - Give 2 cues (where to put hands, where to look, etc.).
  - Hold for 10 seconds.
- Swap.
- Then change partners and rehearse again. (Aim for 3 different partners.)

### Wrap (5 min)

- 2 children share: *"What was the best feedback I got?"*

### Close (2 min)

Tāḍāsana. Namaste.

## Homework

- Practise your teaching ONE more time at home. Tomorrow is the showcase!

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Teach without your drawing — from memory.
- **Tier down:** Read off your drawing if you need to.

## Teacher's quick notes

- The 1-minute teach is the actual project. Practice the timing.
- If a child is too shy to teach alone, they can co-teach with a buddy.
