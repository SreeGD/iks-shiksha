# Day 10 — Pose Showcase

**Module:** Yoga (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min (extend if needed)

## Goal

Every child teaches their invented pose to the class for 1 minute.

## Materials

- Mats laid out
- Stickers
- A bell / chime to mark 1 minute

## The flow

### Settle + opening tāḍāsana (3 min)

The biggest, most attentive mountain of the module.

### Pose Showcase (24 min)

- 1 child at a time.
- 1 minute each (chime when time's up).
- Each child:
  1. Says their pose name.
  2. Shows the pose.
  3. Class tries it for 10 seconds.
- Class claps. Sticker.

With ~24 children in 24 minutes, plan tight pacing OR extend into a buffer period.

### Closing ceremony (3 min)

- Whole class in tāḍāsana.
- Teacher: *"You've all learned 5 poses, 1 breath, and INVENTED your own. You're yoga teachers now."*
- Final namaste.

## Homework

- None. Module complete.

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** A child who finishes can lead the class in mountain pose during the closing.
- **Tier down:** Co-teach with a buddy if too shy alone.

## Teacher's quick notes

- This is celebration day. Every pose is a success.
- Take photos of every pose; send to parents.
- Mark the chime gently — children sometimes need 90 seconds, that's fine.
