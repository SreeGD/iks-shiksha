# Day 8 — Project Day 1

**Module:** Yoga (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Each child finalises their invented pose, draws it, and names it.

## Materials

- Drawing paper + crayons
- Mats

## The flow

### Settle + opening tāḍāsana (3 min)

### Project work (20 min)

- Each child decides their final pose name.
- Draws a clear picture of the pose.
- Writes: pose name, what part of the body it stretches/strengthens.
- Practises the pose 3 times.

### Buddy check (5 min)

- Find a partner. Show your pose. Partner tries it. Partner says ONE thing that worked.

### Close (2 min)

Tāḍāsana. Namaste.

## Homework

- Practise teaching your pose to a parent at home. Get them to try it!

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Write 3 sentences of instructions for your pose.
- **Tier down:** Just a drawing + the name is enough.

## Teacher's quick notes

- Confirm pose safety for every child. Redirect any unsafe poses now.
- Some children will struggle to invent — let them adapt an existing pose with a new name.
