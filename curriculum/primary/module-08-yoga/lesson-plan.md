# Module 8 (Primary) — 10-Day Lesson Plan

## At a glance (30-min sessions)

| Day | Title | Pose / Practice | Animal cue |
|-----|-------|------------------|------------|
| 1 | What is Yoga? | tāḍāsana | mountain |
| 2 | The Tree | vṛkṣāsana | flamingo |
| 3 | Child's Rest | balāsana | sleeping puppy |
| 4 | Cat-Cow | mārjārī-bidalāsana | cat + cow |
| 5 | Bumblebee Breath | bhrāmarī | buzzing bee |
| 6 | Animal Yoga Walk | All 5 in a flow | — |
| 7 | Invent a Pose | Creative time | child's own |
| 8 | Project Day 1 | Design + name | — |
| 9 | Project Day 2 | Practise teaching | — |
| 10 | Pose Showcase | Teach your pose | — |

## Daily structure (30 min)

| Time | What |
|------|------|
| 3 min | Settle on mat, 5 breaths |
| 5 min | Story / new pose introduction |
| 12 min | Practice |
| 5 min | Reflection / animal sounds / fun |
| 5 min | Tāḍāsana close + namaste |

## What children leave with

- 5 safe poses they can do daily at home.
- Sanskrit words *yoga, āsana, prāṇa* — recognised, not memorised.
- Bumblebee breath as a calming tool.
- One invented pose with a name.
- The habit of starting and ending with the mountain.
