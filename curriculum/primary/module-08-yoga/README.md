# Module 8 — Yoga for Little Ones

**Age band:** Primary · **Grades:** 3–5 · **Duration:** 10 sessions × 30 min (with ~12 min movement)

## What children will learn

1. Five safe yoga poses: tree, mountain, child's rest, cat-cow, bumblebee.
2. The Sanskrit words *yoga, āsana, prāṇa*.
3. How to breathe slowly and count breaths.
4. That moving and breathing together makes the body feel calm or excited.
5. To invent and name their own yoga pose.

## How this module sounds

- Lots of movement. Animal sounds. Giggles allowed.
- One Sanskrit word per day, repeated as a chant.
- Eyes closed only if children are comfortable; eyes-lowered is fine.

## NEP 2020 alignment

- §4.6 — Indian Knowledge Systems
- §4.31 — Physical fitness, well-being
- §1.4 — Foundational holistic learning

## Materials

- One mat (or clean floor square) per child
- Comfortable clothing
- Wall picture of the 5 poses (with animal cues)

## Safety floor — non-negotiable

- **No** Sūrya Namaskāra at this band (too complex).
- **No** inversions, no backbends beyond gentle cat-cow.
- **No** breath retention. Natural breath only.
- **No** competitive holding.
- **Always** an opt-out: child can sit out any pose, no questions.

## Day-by-day

| Day | Title | Focus |
|-----|-------|-------|
| 1 | What is Yoga? | Story + tāḍāsana (mountain) |
| 2 | The Tree (vṛkṣāsana) | Standing balance, animal cue (flamingo) |
| 3 | Rest like a Child (balāsana) | Calming pose |
| 4 | Cat-Cow | Spine wave + breath |
| 5 | Bumblebee Breath (bhrāmarī) | Humming breath |
| 6 | Animal Yoga Walk | All five poses in a flow |
| 7 | Invent a Pose | Each child creates one |
| 8 | Project Day 1 | Design & name your pose |
| 9 | Project Day 2 | Practise teaching it |
| 10 | Pose Showcase | Each child teaches their pose |

## Project

Each child invents ONE yoga pose, gives it a Sanskrit-sounding name, and teaches the class for 1 minute on Day 10.

See `assessments/project-brief.md`.
