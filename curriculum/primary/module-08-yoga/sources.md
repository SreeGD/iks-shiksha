# Sources — Module 8 Yoga (Primary)

Teacher reference only.

## Source for the poses

- Standard primary-school yoga curricula (e.g. Yoga Alliance children's programmes).
- The five chosen poses are the safest, most accessible for Grade 3–5.

## Source for the Sanskrit names + framework

- Patañjali, *Yoga Sūtras* (c. 2nd c. BCE – 4th c. CE) — the eight-limbed framework.
- For deeper background see the Middle-band Module 8 `.rag-cache/` and `sources.md`.

## For families who ask

- We teach yoga as physical practice with attention to breath. Religious framing is honoured but not centred.
- For deeper exploration of yoga's philosophy, see the Middle-band module.
