# Yoga for Little Ones

Primary · Grades 3–5

---

# What is yoga?

The Sanskrit word *yoga* means "to join."

We join three things together:
- **Body** (movement)
- **Breath**
- **Mind** (attention)

---

# Our Five Poses

1. **Mountain** (tāḍāsana)
2. **Tree** (vṛkṣāsana) — like a flamingo!
3. **Child** (balāsana) — like a sleeping puppy
4. **Cat-Cow** (mārjārī-bidalāsana)
5. **Bumblebee Breath** (bhrāmarī)

---

# Mountain (tāḍāsana)

Stand tall. Feet rooted.

Pretend a string is pulling the top of your head up.

Breathe like a calm mountain.

---

# Tree (vṛkṣāsana) — Safety

Foot above OR below the standing knee.

**NEVER** on the knee.

Wobble is OK. Falling is OK. Try again!

---

# Child's pose (balāsana)

Knees wide. Forehead to mat (or fists).

This is REST. Slow breath.

Anytime you feel tired — child's pose.

---

# Cat-Cow

Hands and knees.

**Inhale → cow** (belly drops, head up)
**Exhale → cat** (back rounds, chin tucks)

Moo and meow are allowed!

---

# Bumblebee Breath

Sit cross-legged.

Breathe in. Hum out — *"Mmmmmmm"*.

Feel the buzz in your head.

(Skip if you have asthma — just breathe normally.)

---

# The Animal Yoga Walk

All five together:

Mountain → Flamingo → Sleeping Puppy → Cat-Cow → Bumblebee → Mountain

About 4 minutes.

---

# Invent your own pose

The rules:
- SAFE (no jumping, no headstands)
- HOLDABLE for 10 seconds
- Has a NAME

The name can be anything — animal, Sanskrit-sounding, made-up.

---

# Your project (Days 7–10)

- Day 7: invent

Sticker for everyone!

---

# Safety reminders

- Knees soft in tree pose
- Forehead can rest on fists if it doesn't reach the mat
- Bumblebee = humming only, no holding breath
- Opt out any time

---

# What we don't do at this age

- No headstands or shoulder stands
- No big backbends
- No breath holding
- No competing

We're just having fun and moving!
