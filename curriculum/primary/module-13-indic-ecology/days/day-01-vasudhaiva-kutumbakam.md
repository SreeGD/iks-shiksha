# Day 1 — Vasudhaiva Kuṭumbakam

**Module:** Indic Ecology — Vasudhaiva Kuṭumbakam (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Identify the source verse and explain the principle.

## Materials

- Notebook, pencils, drawing paper
- (See lesson-plan.md for any day-specific materials)

## The flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

Daily chant or breathing exercise.

### Story recall (4 min)

Briefly recall yesterday's lesson by asking: *"What's one thing you remember from yesterday?"*

### Today's idea (10 min)

- *Vasudhaiva Kuṭumbakam* (IAST: Vasudhaiva Kuṭumbakam; lit. ""the world is one family" — Mahā Upaniṣad 6.71")

- Today's BIG idea: *Vasudhaiva Kuṭumbakam* — "The whole world is one family."

Show a globe. *"All of us — and all animals, all plants, all rivers — are part of ONE big family."*

Children make a "family tree" with themselves at the centre, then add: parents, siblings, neighbours, then a pet, a tree, a river. Each gets a small drawing.

### Activity (8 min)

A simplified version of *Sacred Grove (or Local Ecology) Mapping* (see `activities/`). Draw, sing, or act it out.

### Wrap-up (3 min)

Each child shares one word that describes today.
