# Day 2 — Five Elements as Ecological Frame

**Module:** Indic Ecology — Vasudhaiva Kuṭumbakam (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Link Module 2 pañcabhūta to ecology — interconnection.

## Materials

- Notebook, pencils, drawing paper
- (See lesson-plan.md for any day-specific materials)

## The flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

Daily chant or breathing exercise.

### Story recall (4 min)

Briefly recall yesterday's lesson by asking: *"What's one thing you remember from yesterday?"*

### Today's idea (10 min)

- *yajña* (IAST: yajña; lit. "cycle of giving and receiving (BG 3.10–14)")

- The 5 elements are all FAMILY too!
- Earth, water, fire, air, space — they need each other.
- If we pollute water, earth gets hurt. If we cut trees, air gets hurt.

Children draw a circle of friends: 🌍 💧 🔥 💨 🌌 (sun = fire, etc.). Connect them with lines. *"All friends, all family!"*

### Activity (8 min)

A simplified version of *Sacred Grove (or Local Ecology) Mapping* (see `activities/`). Draw, sing, or act it out.

### Wrap-up (3 min)

Each child shares one word that describes today.
