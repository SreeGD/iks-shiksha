# Day 6 — Pṛthivī Sūkta (AV 12.1)

**Module:** Indic Ecology — Vasudhaiva Kuṭumbakam (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Read the Earth-mother hymn and discuss imagery.

## Materials

- Notebook, pencils, drawing paper
- (See lesson-plan.md for any day-specific materials)

## The flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

Daily chant or breathing exercise.

### Story recall (4 min)

Briefly recall yesterday's lesson by asking: *"What's one thing you remember from yesterday?"*

### Today's idea (10 min)

- *Vasudhaiva Kuṭumbakam* (IAST: Vasudhaiva Kuṭumbakam; lit. ""the world is one family" — Mahā Upaniṣad 6.71")

- An old, old song says: *"The Earth is my Mother!"*

Show the words on the board: *"mātā bhūmiḥ putro ahaṁ pṛthivyāḥ"*

*"Long ago, people sang to the Earth like she was a mother. They asked: please give us food. And they said: I will care for you."*

Children draw the Earth with a smiling face. Around the edge, they draw what the Earth gives us (trees, water, food). Inside their drawing, they write one promise: "I will _______."

### Activity (8 min)

A simplified version of *Sacred Grove (or Local Ecology) Mapping* (see `activities/`). Draw, sing, or act it out.

### Wrap-up (3 min)

Each child shares one word that describes today.
