# Day 8 — Climate Change — The Crisis

**Module:** Indic Ecology — Vasudhaiva Kuṭumbakam (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Summarise key IPCC findings; what is changing and why.

## Materials

- Notebook, pencils, drawing paper
- (See lesson-plan.md for any day-specific materials)

## The flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

Daily chant or breathing exercise.

### Story recall (4 min)

Briefly recall yesterday's lesson by asking: *"What's one thing you remember from yesterday?"*

### Today's idea (10 min)

- *ahiṁsā* (IAST: ahiṁsā; lit. "non-violence; extended to non-human life")

- The Earth is getting warmer because we burn too much fuel.
- Ice is melting. Some animals lose their homes. Some farms have trouble.

Show simple graphics: a thermometer going up, an ice cube melting, a tree being planted.

*"What can WE do?"* Children brainstorm: walk instead of drive, plant trees, save water, don't waste food. Pick one to try this week.

### Activity (8 min)

A simplified version of *Sacred Grove (or Local Ecology) Mapping* (see `activities/`). Draw, sing, or act it out.

### Wrap-up (3 min)

Each child shares one word that describes today.
