# Day 4 — Building a Tithi Calendar

**Module:** Moon Phases and Tithi (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Construct a tithi calendar for the current month using observation and computation.

## Materials

- Notebook, pencils, drawing paper
- (See lesson-plan.md for any day-specific materials)

## The flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

Daily chant or breathing exercise.

### Story recall (4 min)

Briefly recall yesterday's lesson by asking: *"What's one thing you remember from yesterday?"*

### Today's idea (10 min)

- *kṛṣṇa pakṣa* (IAST: kṛṣṇa pakṣa; lit. "waning fortnight (full → new)")

- Let's make a moon calendar for this month!

Show a printed month calendar. Together, look up today's tithi. Mark it with a moon-sticker.

Each child gets a worksheet with 28 boxes (one per day). They draw the moon shape they expect to see each evening, going from now to a month from now. Tomorrow we'll check and correct.

### Activity (8 min)

A simplified version of *10-Day Moon Observation Diary* (see `activities/`). Draw, sing, or act it out.

### Wrap-up (3 min)

Each child shares one word that describes today.
