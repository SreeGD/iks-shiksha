# Day 5 — Lunar Months and Festivals

**Module:** Moon Phases and Tithi (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Map the 12 lunar months (caitra, vaiśākha, ...) to major festivals.

## Materials

- Notebook, pencils, drawing paper
- (See lesson-plan.md for any day-specific materials)

## The flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

Daily chant or breathing exercise.

### Story recall (4 min)

Briefly recall yesterday's lesson by asking: *"What's one thing you remember from yesterday?"*

### Today's idea (10 min)

- *tithi* (IAST: tithi; lit. "lunar day (1/30 of synodic month)")

Introduce the concept gently using a story, a drawing, or a sensory example. The concept for today: **Lunar Months and Festivals**. Keep sentences short.

### Activity (8 min)

A simplified version of *10-Day Moon Observation Diary* (see `activities/`). Draw, sing, or act it out.

### Wrap-up (3 min)

Each child shares one word that describes today.
