# Day 10 — Synthesis and Quiz

**Module:** Moon Phases and Tithi (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Final quiz and 10-day moon-diary share.

## Materials

- Notebook, pencils, drawing paper
- (See lesson-plan.md for any day-specific materials)

## The flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

Daily chant or breathing exercise.

### Story recall (4 min)

Briefly recall yesterday's lesson by asking: *"What's one thing you remember from yesterday?"*

### Today's idea (10 min)

- *pakṣa* (IAST: pakṣa; lit. "fortnight (half-month)")

- Today we celebrate everything we learned about the Moon!

Each child shares ONE thing from their moon diary. *"Did the Moon look how you thought it would?"*

Make a class moon-mural: a circle with all the children's moon drawings stuck around the outside. Sing the tithi rhyme one more time.

Take the quiz with stickers (not stress) — every child gets a "Moon Watcher" sticker.

### Activity (8 min)

A simplified version of *10-Day Moon Observation Diary* (see `activities/`). Draw, sing, or act it out.

### Wrap-up (3 min)

Each child shares one word that describes today.
