# Module 9 — Subtraction by Nikhilam (Darśana)

**Sanskrit:** *Nikhilaṁ Navataścaramaṁ Daśataḥ* · **Band:** Primary (3-5) · **Duration:** 60 min · **Path:** Darśana (Workshop)

"All from 9, last from 10" — a complement method for subtraction.

> **Darśana note.** This workshop condenses Module 9's full 10-day arc into one hour. For the deeper version — daily lesson plans, quizzes, assessments, slide decks — switch to **[Adhyayana](../)** (the Full Course path).

---

## Opening hook · 5 min

Write 100 − 7 on the board. *"Quick — say the answer!"* (93.) *"What about 100 − 37?"* (Slower.) *"Today: a trick that makes 100 − ANYTHING fast."*

---

## Core idea · 15 min

**The trick: "All from 9, last from 10"**

For 100 − 37:
- The "9 column" digit (3): 9 − 3 = 6
- The "10 column" digit (7): 10 − 7 = 3
- Answer: 63!

Chant: *"All from 9, last from 10!"*

---

## Demonstration · 10 min

Chorus through 5 problems: 100 − 25, 100 − 47, 100 − 89, 100 − 91, 100 − 56. Children call out the answers.

---

## Source moment · 5 min

Tell the story: *"All from 9, last from 10!"* This little rule helps subtract big numbers fast.

---

## Hands-on activity · 15 min

Worksheet: 10 problems of (100 − X), where X is 2-digit. Pairs check. Stars for completion.

---

## Reflection + take-home · 10 min

Each child does one Nikhilam problem aloud. Cheer.
