# Module 7 — ×11 Multiplication (Darśana)

**Sanskrit:** *Ekādhikena Pūrveṇa* · **Band:** Primary (3-5) · **Duration:** 60 min · **Path:** Darśana (Workshop)

Mental multiplication by 11 from 20th-century Indian arithmetic.

## Opening hook · 5 min

Write 23 × 11 on the board. Pause. *"Anyone solve it in 3 seconds?"* (Pause.) *"Today's magic trick: split the digits, add them, put the sum in middle. 23 → 2_3 → 2_(2+3)_3 → 253. Magic!"*

---

## Core idea · 15 min

**The trick for ×11:**

For any 2-digit number, split the digits and put their sum in the middle.

24 → 2_4 → 2_(2+4)_4 → 264.
35 → 3_5 → 3_(3+5)_5 → 385.
71 → 7_1 → 7_(7+1)_1 → 781.

Chant: *"Split, add, put in middle!"*

---

## Demonstration · 10 min

Practice in chorus: 12 × 11, 21 × 11, 34 × 11, 56 × 11. Chant the answers together.

---

## Source moment · 5 min

Tell the story of a wise teacher who said: *"Numbers like games. The ×11 game has a simple rule. Today we play."*

---

## Hands-on activity · 15 min

Each child solves 5 ×11 problems on a worksheet. Pairs check. Cheer for completed worksheets.

---

## Reflection + take-home · 10 min

Each child says one ×11 problem they solved. Cheer for each.
