# Day 1 — The Hook — What Is the Trick?

**Module:** ×11 Multiplication — Ekādhikena Pūrveṇa (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Compute 23×11, 35×11 mentally using the split-and-sum method.

## Materials

- Notebook, pencils, drawing paper
- (See lesson-plan.md for any day-specific materials)

## The flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

Daily chant or breathing exercise.

### Story recall (4 min)

Briefly recall yesterday's lesson by asking: *"What's one thing you remember from yesterday?"*

### Today's idea (10 min)

- *Ekādhikena Pūrveṇa* (IAST: Ekādhikena Pūrveṇa; lit. ""by one more than the previous" — sūtra 1 in Tirthaji")

- Watch this trick! 23 × 11 = ?

Show on the board: "2 _ 3" with a gap. Whisper: "What's 2 + 3?" (Wait for "5".) Fill in: "2 5 3 → 253!"

Each child practices: 12 × 11 = 132. 34 × 11 = 374. 25 × 11 = 275. Try 5 more.

Magic chant: *"Split, add, put in middle!"* (Children chant 3 times.)

### Activity (8 min)

A simplified version of *Mental Math Sprint Cards* (see `activities/`). Draw, sing, or act it out.

### Wrap-up (3 min)

Each child shares one word that describes today.
