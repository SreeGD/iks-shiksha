## Pacing notes

- Sessions are ~30 minutes. Be flexible — Days 3 and 4 can each stretch if children are still trying combinations.
- The chant ("all to fifteen, all to fifteen") is sung at the start of every class from Day 2 onwards. Repetition is the lesson.
- Days 1–5 are the math; Days 6–8 add story and culture; Days 9–10 are the project.

## Daily chant

> *"One, two, three — count to fifteen!
> Four, five, six — still fifteen!
> Seven, eight, nine — all to fifteen!"*

(Use during settle-in. Clap on each number.)

## What children leave with

- A coloured 3×3 magic square they made themselves.
- The chant "all to fifteen."
- Two stories: the **Lo Shu turtle** and the **Khajuraho stone**.
- Confidence adding three single-digit numbers.
