# Day 6 — 4×4 and the Khajuraho Square

**Module:** Magic Squares — Bhadragaṇita (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Examine the Khajuraho inscription and its panel-magic property.

## Materials

- Notebook, pencils, drawing paper
- (See lesson-plan.md for any day-specific materials)

## The flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

Daily chant or breathing exercise.

### Story recall (4 min)

Briefly recall yesterday's lesson by asking: *"What's one thing you remember from yesterday?"*

### Today's idea (10 min)

- *kothumi vidhi* (IAST: kothumi vidhi; lit. "Nārāyaṇa's algorithm for odd-order squares")

Introduce the concept gently using a story, a drawing, or a sensory example. The concept for today: **4×4 and the Khajuraho Square**. Keep sentences short.

### Activity (8 min)

A simplified version of *Class 3×3 Magic-Square Tournament* (see `activities/`). Draw, sing, or act it out.

### Wrap-up (3 min)

Each child shares one word that describes today.
