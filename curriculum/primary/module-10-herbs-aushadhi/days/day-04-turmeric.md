# Day 4 — Turmeric — Golden Root

**Module:** Herbs — Auṣadhi (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Distinguish fresh root vs powder; identify curcumin as active compound.

## Materials

- Notebook, pencils, drawing paper
- (See lesson-plan.md for any day-specific materials)

## The flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

Daily chant or breathing exercise.

### Story recall (4 min)

Briefly recall yesterday's lesson by asking: *"What's one thing you remember from yesterday?"*

### Today's idea (10 min)

- *haridrā* (IAST: haridrā; lit. "turmeric — *Curcuma longa*")

Introduce the concept gently using a story, a drawing, or a sensory example. The concept for today: **Turmeric — Golden Root**. Keep sentences short.

### Activity (8 min)

A simplified version of *Blind Smell-Test Identification* (see `activities/`). Draw, sing, or act it out.

### Wrap-up (3 min)

Each child shares one word that describes today.
