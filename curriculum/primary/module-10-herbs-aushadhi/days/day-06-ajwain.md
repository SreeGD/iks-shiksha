# Day 6 — Ajwain — Carom Seed

**Module:** Herbs — Auṣadhi (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Recognise seeds and leaves; identify thymol; traditional digestive use.

## Materials

- Notebook, pencils, drawing paper
- (See lesson-plan.md for any day-specific materials)

## The flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

Daily chant or breathing exercise.

### Story recall (4 min)

Briefly recall yesterday's lesson by asking: *"What's one thing you remember from yesterday?"*

### Today's idea (10 min)

- *yavānī* (IAST: yavānī; lit. "ajwain — *Trachyspermum ammi*")

- Today: meet Ajwain (Yavānī)! 🌿

Show ajwain seeds. *"Tiny, like baby caraway."* Smell them — strong! sharp!

*"People chew a few seeds after a big meal to help their tummy."*

Children draw the seeds (small dots) and write "yavānī" beside.

### Activity (8 min)

A simplified version of *Blind Smell-Test Identification* (see `activities/`). Draw, sing, or act it out.

### Wrap-up (3 min)

Each child shares one word that describes today.
