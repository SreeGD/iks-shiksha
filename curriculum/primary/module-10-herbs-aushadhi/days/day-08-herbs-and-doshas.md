# Day 8 — Herbs and the Doṣas

**Module:** Herbs — Auṣadhi (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Categorise the six herbs by heating/cooling effect (links to Module 4).

## Materials

- Notebook, pencils, drawing paper
- (See lesson-plan.md for any day-specific materials)

## The flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

Daily chant or breathing exercise.

### Story recall (4 min)

Briefly recall yesterday's lesson by asking: *"What's one thing you remember from yesterday?"*

### Today's idea (10 min)

- *auṣadhi* (IAST: auṣadhi; lit. "medicinal plant / herb")

- Each herb has a "temperature" inside us:
  - **Warm herbs:** ginger, ajwain (good when we feel cold/sick)
  - **Cool herbs:** mint, sometimes turmeric (good when we feel hot)

Match game: show pictures of the 6 herbs. Children sort into "warm" pile and "cool" pile.

*"Warm herbs help in winter. Cool herbs help in summer."*

### Activity (8 min)

A simplified version of *Blind Smell-Test Identification* (see `activities/`). Draw, sing, or act it out.

### Wrap-up (3 min)

Each child shares one word that describes today.
