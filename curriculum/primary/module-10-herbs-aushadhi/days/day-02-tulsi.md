# Day 2 — Tulsī — Holy Basil

**Module:** Herbs — Auṣadhi (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Identify tulasī by leaf and smell; name the active compound (eugenol).

## Materials

- Notebook, pencils, drawing paper
- (See lesson-plan.md for any day-specific materials)

## The flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

Daily chant or breathing exercise.

### Story recall (4 min)

Briefly recall yesterday's lesson by asking: *"What's one thing you remember from yesterday?"*

### Today's idea (10 min)

- *tulasī* (IAST: tulasī; lit. "holy basil — *Ocimum tenuiflorum*")

- Today: meet Tulsī! 🌿

Show a tulsī plant (or picture + dried leaf).
- Smell it. *"What does it remind you of?"* (Spice. Mint. Tea.)
- Tulsī tea is sometimes used when people feel a cold coming.

Children draw a tulsī leaf and write "tulasī" beside it.

### Activity (8 min)

A simplified version of *Blind Smell-Test Identification* (see `activities/`). Draw, sing, or act it out.

### Wrap-up (3 min)

Each child shares one word that describes today.
