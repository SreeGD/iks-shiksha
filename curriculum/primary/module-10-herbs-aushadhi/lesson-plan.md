# Lesson Plan — Herbs — Auṣadhi (Primary)

10-day arc, 30 min per session.

### Day 1 — What is Auṣadhi?

**Objective:** Define auṣadhi and recognise the six herbs of this module.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (15 min) · Activity (8 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 2 — Tulsī — Holy Basil

**Objective:** Identify tulasī by leaf and smell; name the active compound (eugenol).

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (15 min) · Activity (8 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 3 — Neem — Bitter Healer

**Objective:** Identify nimba; learn about azadirachtin; understand high-dose safety warning.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (15 min) · Activity (8 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 4 — Turmeric — Golden Root

**Objective:** Distinguish fresh root vs powder; identify curcumin as active compound.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (15 min) · Activity (8 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 5 — Ginger — Warming Root

**Objective:** Distinguish fresh vs dried; gingerol vs shogaol.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (15 min) · Activity (8 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 6 — Ajwain — Carom Seed

**Objective:** Recognise seeds and leaves; identify thymol; traditional digestive use.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (15 min) · Activity (8 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 7 — Mint — Cooling Leaf

**Objective:** Recognise several Mentha varieties; identify menthol.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (15 min) · Activity (8 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 8 — Herbs and the Doṣas

**Objective:** Categorise the six herbs by heating/cooling effect (links to Module 4).

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (15 min) · Activity (8 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 9 — Build the Class Deck

**Objective:** Each student contributes one identification card to the class library.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (15 min) · Activity (8 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

### Day 10 — Final Share and Assessment

**Objective:** Present the class herb deck; take summative quiz.

**Flow:** Warm-up (5 min) · Core (15 min) · Activity (8 min) · Wrap-up (5 min)

## Pacing notes

- Primary sessions are 30 min. Adjust if your block is different.
- Days 5 and 9 are buffer days — use to reinforce weaker concepts or extend stronger ones.
- The summative project (see `assessments/project-brief.md`) runs in parallel from Day 6 onward.

## Differentiation

- **For advanced students:** see `teacher-notes.md` for extension prompts.
- **For students needing more support:** simplify activities, do more pair-work, allow oral instead of written responses.
