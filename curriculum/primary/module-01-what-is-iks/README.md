# Module 1 — Five Gifts from India (What is IKS?)

**Age band:** Primary · **Grades:** 3–5 · **Duration:** 10 sessions × 30 min

## What children will learn

Five "gifts" — Indian contributions to the world that they use or hear about every day:

1. **Zero** — the powerful number that makes counting big numbers possible.
2. **Yoga** — moving and breathing together.
3. **Ayurveda** — the old Indian way of staying healthy.
4. **Vedic Math** — fast counting tricks.
5. **Indian classical music** — special sounds called *rāga*.

By Day 10 they can name all 5 gifts and tell a 30-second story about one.

## How this module sounds

- One story per day.
- A daily chant of the five gifts.
- Drawing + show-and-tell, not paper quizzes.

## Materials

- Drawing paper, crayons
- 5 picture cards (one per "gift")
- Stickers

## Day-by-day

| Day | Title | Activity |
|-----|-------|----------|
| 1 | Five Gifts Story | Meet all 5 + chant |
| 2 | The Magic Zero | Draw big numbers with and without zero |
| 3 | Yoga Gift | One pose, one breath |
| 4 | Ayurveda — Three Friends | Three doshas as friends |
| 5 | Five-Gift Song | Sing and recall |
| 6 | The Math Story | Indian math through a story |
| 7 | Sky Watchers | Indian astronomy — Aryabhata story |
| 8 | Project Day 1 | Pick + draw |
| 9 | Project Day 2 | Finish + practise |
| 10 | Show & Tell | Each child shares for 30 sec |
