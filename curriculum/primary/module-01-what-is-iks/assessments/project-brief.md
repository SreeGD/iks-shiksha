# Project — Module 1 (Primary): Five Gifts

## What you'll make

Pick ONE of the 5 gifts (Zero, Yoga, Ayurveda, Vedic Math, Music). Make:
- A poster, OR
- A 3D model (clay or recycled junk), OR
- A 30-second story.

## Schedule

- Day 7: pick your gift.
- Day 8: start.
- Day 9: finish + practise.
- Day 10: Show & Tell, 30 seconds each.

## Stars

| | What we celebrate |
|---|---|
| ⭐ | You named your gift |
| ⭐⭐ | You showed it clearly |
| ⭐⭐⭐ | You told us WHY you picked it |

All children get a sticker.
