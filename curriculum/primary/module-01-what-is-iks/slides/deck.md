---

# Gift 1: Zero

The most powerful number!

Without zero we couldn't write 10, 100, 1000.

India invented zero as a number ~1500 years ago.

---

# Gift 2: Yoga

Moving with breath.

Yoga = "to join."

Body + breath + mind.

---

# Gift 3: Ayurveda

The old Indian way of staying healthy.

Three friends in your body: Vāta (wind), Pitta (fire), Kapha (water).

---

# Gift 4: Vedic Math

Fast counting tricks!

Like: 23 × 11 = 253. (Split, add, put in the middle.)

---

# Gift 5: Indian Classical Music

Special sounds called *rāga*.

Different rāgas for different times of day.

---

# Aryabhata's big idea

He said: WE spin, not the sky.

He was right!

He was 1000 years before anyone in Europe figured this out.
