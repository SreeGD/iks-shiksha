# Module 1 (Primary) — 10-Day Lesson Plan

| Day | Title | Activity |
|-----|-------|----------|
| 1 | Five Gifts Story | Story + meet 5 picture cards |
| 2 | Magic Zero | Count to a million game |
| 3 | Yoga Gift | Tāḍāsana + bumblebee breath |
| 4 | Ayurveda Three Friends | Vāta-Pitta-Kapha picture intro |
| 5 | Five-Gift Song | Memorise via singing |
| 6 | Math Story | Bakhshali manuscript tale |
| 7 | Sky Watchers | Aryabhata story |
| 8 | Project Day 1 | Pick a gift, start drawing |
| 9 | Project Day 2 | Finish + practise |
| 10 | Show & Tell | 30-sec each, sticker for all |

## Daily structure (30 min)

| Time | What |
|------|------|
| 3 min | Settle + the daily chant |
| 10 min | Story/new content |
| 12 min | Drawing/hands-on |
| 5 min | Sharing |

## Daily chant

> "Zero! Yoga! Ayurveda! Vedic Math! Music!"
>
> "Five gifts from India to the whole wide world!"

## What children leave with

- Five gifts named confidently
- A drawing of their favourite gift
- A 30-second story to share with a younger sibling
