# Activity 01 — Five Gifts Memory Cards

**Module:** Five Gifts (Primary) · **Day:** 1 / reusable · **Time:** 10 min

## Goal

Children match each gift name to its picture.

## Materials per pair

- 10 cards: 5 with gift names (Zero, Yoga, Ayurveda, Vedic Math, Music), 5 with simple pictures

## Rules

Memory match — face-down grid, flip 2 cards at a time, keep pairs.

## Discussion

- Which gift did you remember easiest? Why?
