# Teacher Notes — Module 1 (Primary)

## Tone

Story first. Drawing always. No paper tests.

## What to celebrate

If by Day 10 every child can:
- Name 3 of the 5 gifts
- Point to one example in their own life

...the module is a success. Everything else is bonus.

## Common things to handle

1. **"Where is football?"** It came from England. Some gifts come from India, some from elsewhere. That's fine.
2. **"My grandma uses Ayurveda."** Celebrate! Invite the child to share a family example.
3. **"Why is zero a gift? We learn it in school."** Yes — and Indians invented the way of using it. Without that invention, school math would look very different.

## Safety

- No real fire, no real surgery (we don't get into Suśruta at this age!), no breath holding.

## If you only have 6 days

- Day 1: Five Gifts story
- Day 2: Zero + Yoga (combined)
- Day 3: Ayurveda + Music
- Day 4: Math + Astronomy stories
- Day 5: Project day
- Day 6: Show & Tell
