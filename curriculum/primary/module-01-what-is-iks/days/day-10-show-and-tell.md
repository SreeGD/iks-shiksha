# Day 10 — Show & Tell

**Module:** Five Gifts (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min (extend if needed)

## Goal

Each child shares their gift project for 30 seconds. Sticker for all.

## Materials

- Children's projects
- Stickers
- Chime for timing

## Flow

### Settle + chant + song (5 min)

Biggest chant yet!

### Show & Tell (22 min)

- 1 child at a time. 30 seconds. Chime.
- Each child:
  1. Names their gift.
  2. Shows project.
  3. One sentence: why they picked it.

Class claps. Sticker.

### Closing (3 min)

- Stand in a circle.
- Sing the song one final time.
- *"You all know the five gifts. They're with you for life."*

## Homework

- None. Module done.
