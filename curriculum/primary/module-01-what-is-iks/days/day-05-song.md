# Day 5 — Five-Gift Song

**Module:** Five Gifts (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Children memorise all 5 gifts via a song.

## Materials

- 5 picture cards (one per gift)

## Flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

### Song (10 min)

Use a familiar tune (Twinkle Twinkle or similar):

> Zero, yoga, ayurveda,
> Vedic math and music too,
> Five great gifts from old, old India,
> Things we use, me and you!

Sing 3 times. Add claps on each gift name.

### Recall game (12 min)

- Hold up cards one at a time. Class shouts the name.
- Turn cards face-down. Shout from memory.
- Mix the order.

### Drawing (3 min)

Quick: each child writes the 5 names on one paper.

### Show + chant (2 min)

- Sing one more time, biggest yet.

## Homework

- Teach the song to a family member.

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Make up a verse 2 of the song.
- **Tier down:** Just sing along — don't worry about every word.
