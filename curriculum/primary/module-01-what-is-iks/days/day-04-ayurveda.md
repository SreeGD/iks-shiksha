# Day 4 — Ayurveda: Three Friends

**Module:** Five Gifts (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Children meet the three doshas (vāta, pitta, kapha) as "three friends in your body."

## Materials

- 3 picture cards: wind blowing leaves (Vāta), fire/sun (Pitta), water/clouds (Kapha)
- Drawing paper

## Flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

### Story (10 min)

*"Ayurveda is the OLD Indian way of staying healthy. It started about 2000 years ago."*

*"Ayurveda says everyone's body has THREE FRIENDS inside it. We all have all three — but everyone has a special favourite."*

Show the cards:

**Vāta (wind friend):**
- Moves things around inside you.
- People with lots of Vāta are quick, talkative, sometimes fidgety.

**Pitta (fire friend):**
- Makes your food into energy.
- People with lots of Pitta are sharp, focused, can be a bit fiery.

**Kapha (water/earth friend):**
- Keeps you strong and steady.
- People with lots of Kapha are calm, patient, sometimes slow.

*"None of these is 'good' or 'bad' — they're all friends. The trick is keeping them in balance."*

### Drawing (12 min)

Each child draws their three friends. They can give them faces, names, anything!

### Show + chant (3 min)

- 2 shares.
- Chant.

### Wrap-up (2 min)

- *"Tomorrow we sing about all 5 gifts together!"*

## Homework

- Ask a parent: which of the three friends do they think is YOUR favourite?

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Make up a story about the three friends having a small argument and making up.
- **Tier down:** Draw just one friend.

## Teacher notes

- Module 3 (Ayurveda + Good Health) is the FULL Ayurveda module. Today is a taste.
- Don't push children to identify their "type" — at this age it's just an introduction.
