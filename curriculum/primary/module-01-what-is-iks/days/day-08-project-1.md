# Day 8 — Project Day 1

**Module:** Five Gifts (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Each child picks ONE gift and starts a project.

## Materials

- Drawing paper, crayons, recycled materials, clay

## Flow

### Settle + chant + song (5 min)

### Project choice (5 min)

Each child picks ONE gift + format:
- A poster
- A 3D model (clay or recycled)
- A 30-second story

### Project work (17 min)

Quiet work. Teacher rotates: 60 sec per child, ONE question: *"What's your favourite part?"*

### Show + chant (3 min)

## Homework

- Finish at home if not done in class. Practise telling about it 2 times.
