# Day 1 — Five Gifts Story

**Module:** Five Gifts from India (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Children meet all 5 gifts and learn the daily chant.

## Materials

- 5 picture cards (zero, yoga pose, herbs/spices, math symbols, vīṇā or other Indian instrument)
- Drawing paper

## Flow

### Settle + chant intro (5 min)

Sit on mat. Teach the chant:

> "Zero! Yoga! Ayurveda! Vedic Math! Music!"
>
> "Five gifts from India to the whole wide world!"

Say it 3 times.

### Story (10 min)

*"A long, long time ago — about 2000 years ago — thinkers in India invented some amazing things. They shared these with the whole world. Today we meet FIVE of them."*

Hold up each card:
- **Card 1: the number 0.** *"Imagine trying to write 'one hundred' without zero. You couldn't. India gave us zero."*
- **Card 2: yoga pose.** *"Standing tall like a mountain — that's a yoga pose called tāḍāsana. India invented yoga."*
- **Card 3: spices / a tulsi plant.** *"Ayurveda is the old Indian way of staying healthy. Things like ginger tea when you have a cold come from this tradition."*
- **Card 4: math symbols.** *"India invented fast ways to do math in your head. We'll meet some this week."*
- **Card 5: an Indian instrument.** *"Indian music has a special structure called rāga — different notes for different times of day."*

### Drawing (12 min)

Each child picks ONE gift and draws it.

### Show + chant (3 min)

2 children show drawings. Class chants the gift name.

## Homework

- Tell a parent about one of the five gifts at dinner tonight.

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Draw all 5 gifts on one page.
- **Tier down:** Pick the gift with the easiest image (zero or yoga pose).
