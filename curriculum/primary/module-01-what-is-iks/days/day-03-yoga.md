# Day 3 — Yoga Gift

**Module:** Five Gifts (Primary) · **Time:** 30 min

## Goal

Children try ONE yoga pose with breath co-ordination.

## Materials

- Floor space (no shoes)

## Flow

### Settle + chant (3 min)

### Story (5 min)

*"Yoga is a very old practice from India. Yoga means 'joining' — joining your body, your breath, and your mind."*

*"Today we try ONE pose: tāḍāsana — mountain pose."*

### Practice (15 min)

- Stand tall. Feet firm on floor.
- *"Pretend a string is gently pulling the top of your head up."*
- *"Take 3 slow breaths."*
- Hold for 30 seconds. Release. Repeat 3 times.

Then introduce **bumblebee breath**:
- Sit cross-legged. Take a slow breath in.
- Breathe out humming — "mmmmmmmm" — like a bumblebee.
- Do 5 times.

### Show + chant (5 min)

- *"How did your body feel after the mountain?"*
- *"How did your head feel after the bumblebee?"*
- Daily chant.

### Wrap-up (2 min)

- *"When you feel angry or sad — try mountain pose for 30 seconds. Or 3 bumblebee breaths. Yoga is a TOOL you can carry with you."*

## Homework

- Stand like a mountain for 30 seconds before brushing your teeth tomorrow.

## Differentiation

- **Tier up:** Add tree pose (vṛkṣāsana).
- **Tier down:** Just do mountain pose, no breath.
